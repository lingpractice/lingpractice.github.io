a(1)
: the hollow horny shaft of a feather see feather illustration
(2)
: feather

b
: one of the hollow sharp spines of a porcupine or hedgehog
c
: pen entry 3 sense 3
3
: something made from or resembling the quill of a feather

4
: a float for a fishing line
