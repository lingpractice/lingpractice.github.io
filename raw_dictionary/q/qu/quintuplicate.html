quintuplicate

1 of 3

adjective

quin·​tu·​pli·​cate kwin-ˈtü-pli-kət 
-ˈtyü-
1
: consisting of or existing in five corresponding or identical parts or examples
quintuplicate invoices
2
: being the fifth of five things exactly alike
file the quintuplicate copy

quintuplicate

2 of 3

noun

1
: one of five things exactly alike
specifically : one of five identical copies
2
: five copies all alike
—used with in
typed in quintuplicate

quintuplicate

3 of 3

verb

quin·​tu·​pli·​cate kwin-ˈtü-plə-ˌkāt 
-ˈtyü-
quintuplicated; quintuplicating

transitive verb

1
: to make quintuple or fivefold
2
: to prepare in quintuplicate

Word History

Etymology

Adjective

Medieval Latin quintuplicatus, past participle of quintuplicare to quintuple, from quintuplus quintuple

First Known Use

Adjective

1656, in the meaning defined at sense 1

Noun

1851, in the meaning defined at sense 1

Verb

circa 1889, in the meaning defined at sense 1
