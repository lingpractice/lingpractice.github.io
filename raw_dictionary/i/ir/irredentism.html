irredentism

noun

ir·​re·​den·​tism ˌir-i-ˈden-ˌti-zəm 
: a political principle or policy directed toward the incorporation of irredentas within the boundaries of their historically or ethnically related political unit
irredentist
ˌir-i-ˈden-tist 
noun or adjective
