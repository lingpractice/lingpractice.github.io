idyllic

adjective

idyl·​lic ī-ˈdi-lik 
 chiefly British  i-
1
: pleasing or picturesque in natural simplicity
2
: of, relating to, or being an idyll
idyllically
ī-ˈdi-li-k(ə-)lē

adverb
