inflict

verb

in·​flict in-ˈflikt

Synonyms of inflict

transitive verb

1
a
: to give by or as if by striking
inflict a painful sting
inflict damage
b
: to cause (something unpleasant) to be endured
… inflict my annual message upon the church itself if it might derive benefit thereby.—Mark Twain
2
: afflict
inflicter noun
or inflictor

inflictive
