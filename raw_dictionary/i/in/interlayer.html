interlayer

1 of 2

verb

in·​ter·​lay·​er ˌin-tər-ˈlā-ər 
-ˈler
interlayered; interlayering
1
transitive : to insert (something) between or as if between other layers : to arrange (something) in alternating parts or layers
… this production interlayered scenes from both operas, switching the action back and forth …—Jeremy Eichler
… a kind of rice pudding, interlayered with jam and topped with puff pastry …—Harry Wallop
2
intransitive : to lie or settle in layers between other layers
… as the lenses [of celestite] become larger and more abundant, they interlayer and form a single continuous bed.—Douglas L. Rickman
interlayering noun
plural interlayerings
the interlayering of different sediments
