intervenor

noun

in·​ter·​ve·​nor ˌin-tər-ˈvē-nər 
-ˌnȯr
variants or intervener
ˌin-tər-ˈvē-nər 
: one who intervenes
especially : one who intervenes as a third party in a legal proceeding
