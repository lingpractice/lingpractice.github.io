intrauterine

adjective

in·​tra·​uter·​ine ˌin-trə-ˈyü-tə-rən  -ˌrīn 
-(ˌ)trä-
: situated, used, or occurring within the uterus
intrauterine insemination
also : involving the part of development that takes place in the uterus
intrauterine mortality
