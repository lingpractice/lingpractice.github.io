interlacustrine

adjective

in·​ter·​la·​cus·​trine ˌin-tər-lə-ˈkə-strən 
: of, relating to, or situated in an area between lakes
specifically : of, relating to, or situated in the region of Africa that is bounded by Lakes Victoria, Kyoga, Albert, Edward, and Tanganyika
Uganda is situated in the interlacustrine highlands of central Africa … —Rob Marchant
the early interlacustrine kingdoms
interlacustrine Bantu speakers
