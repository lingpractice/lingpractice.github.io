inlet

noun

in·​let ˈin-ˌlet 
-lət
Synonyms of inlet
1
a
: a bay or recess in the shore of a sea, lake, or river
also : creek
b
: a narrow water passage between peninsulas or through a barrier island leading to a bay or lagoon
2
: a way of entering
especially : an opening for intake
