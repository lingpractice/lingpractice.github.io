indictee

noun

in·​dict·​ee
(ˌ)inˌdītˈē,
ə̇n-
plural -s
: one that is indicted

Word History

Etymology

alteration (influenced by indict entry 1) of enditee, from endite (earlier form of indict entry 1) + -ee
