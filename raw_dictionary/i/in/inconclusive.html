inconclusive

adjective

in·​con·​clu·​sive ˌin-kən-ˈklü-siv 
-ziv
Synonyms of inconclusive
: leading to no conclusion or definite result
inconclusive evidence
an inconclusive argument
inconclusively adverb
inconclusiveness noun
