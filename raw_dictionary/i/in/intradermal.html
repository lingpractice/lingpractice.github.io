intradermal

adjective

in·​tra·​der·​mal ˌin-trə-ˈdər-məl 
-(ˌ)trä-
: situated, occurring, or done within or between the layers of the skin
also : administered by entering the skin
intradermal injections
intradermally
ˌin-trə-ˈdər-mə-lē 
-(ˌ)trä-
