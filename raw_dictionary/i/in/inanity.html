inanity

noun

inan·​i·​ty i-ˈna-nə-tē 
plural inanities
Synonyms of inanity
1
: the quality or state of being inane: such as
a
: vapid, pointless, or fatuous character : shallowness
b
: lack of substance : emptiness
2
: something that is inane
