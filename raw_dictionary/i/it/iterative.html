iterative

adjective

it·​er·​a·​tive ˈi-tə-ˌrā-tiv  -rə- 
: involving repetition: such as
a
: expressing repetition of a verbal action
b
: utilizing the repetition of a sequence of operations or procedures
iterative programming methods
iteratively adverb
