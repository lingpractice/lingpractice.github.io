illiquid

adjective

il·​liq·​uid (ˌ)i(l)-ˈlik-wəd 
1
: not being cash or readily convertible into cash
illiquid holdings
2
: deficient in liquid assets
an illiquid bank
illiquidity
(ˌ)i(l)-ˌli-ˈkwi-də-tē 
noun
