isolationism

noun

iso·​la·​tion·​ism ˌī-sə-ˈlā-shə-ˌni-zəm 
 also  ˌi-
: a policy of national isolation by abstention from alliances and other international political and economic relations
isolationist
ˌī-sə-ˈlā-sh(ə-)nist 
 also  ˌi-
noun or adjective
