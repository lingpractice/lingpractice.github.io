immediacy

noun

im·​me·​di·​a·​cy i-ˈmē-dē-ə-sē 
 British often  -ˈmē-jə-sē
plural immediacies
Synonyms of immediacy
1
: the quality or state of being immediate
2
: something that is immediate
—usually used in plural
