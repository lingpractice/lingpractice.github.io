impressionist

noun

im·​pres·​sion·​ist im-ˈpre-sh(ə-)nist 
Synonyms of impressionist
1
often capitalized : someone (such as a painter) who practices or adheres to the theories of impressionism
2
: an entertainer who does impressions

Synonyms

  • imitator
  • impersonator
  • mimic
  • personator
