impenetrable

adjective

im·​pen·​e·​tra·​ble (ˌ)im-ˈpe-nə-trə-bəl 
Synonyms of impenetrable
1
a
: incapable of being penetrated or pierced
b
: inaccessible to knowledge, reason, or sympathy : impervious
2
: incapable of being comprehended : inscrutable
impenetrably
(ˌ)im-ˈpe-nə-trə-blē 
adverb
