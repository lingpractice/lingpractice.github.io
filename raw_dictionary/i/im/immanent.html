immanent

adjective

im·​ma·​nent ˈi-mə-nənt 
Synonyms of immanent
1
: indwelling, inherent
beauty is not something imposed but something immanent—Anthony Burgess
2
: being within the limits of possible experience or knowledge compare transcendent
