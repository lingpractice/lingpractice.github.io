awry

adverb or adjective

ə-ˈrī 
Synonyms of awry
1
: off the correct or expected course : amiss
Their plans went awry.
2
: in a turned or twisted position or direction : askew
His wig was put on all awry, with the tail straggling about his neck.—Charles Dickens

Synonyms

  • afield
  • amiss
  • astray
  • wrong
