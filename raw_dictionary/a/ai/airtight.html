airtight

adjective

air·​tight ˈer-ˌtīt 
Synonyms of airtight
1
: impermeable to air or nearly so
an airtight seal
2
a
: having no noticeable weakness, flaw, or loophole
an airtight argument
b
: permitting no opportunity for an opponent to score
an airtight defense
airtightness noun
