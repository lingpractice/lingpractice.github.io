ailing

adjective

ail·​ing ˈā-liŋ 
Synonyms of ailing
: having or suffering from an illness or injury
providing care for his ailing mother
trying to rest her ailing knee/back
a person in ailing health
—often used figuratively
an ailing company
an ailing economy

Synonyms

  • invalid
  • sickly
  • weakly
