autocephalous

adjective

au·​to·​ceph·​a·​lous ˌȯ-tō-ˈse-f(ə-)ləs 
: independent of external and especially patriarchal authority
—used especially of Eastern national churches
autocephaly
ˌȯ-tō-ˈse-fə-lē 
noun
