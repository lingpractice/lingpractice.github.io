audiovisual

adjective

au·​dio·​vi·​su·​al ˌȯ-dē-(ˌ)ō-ˈvi-zhə-wəl 
-zhəl,

Synonyms of audiovisual
1
: designed to aid in learning or teaching by making use of both hearing and sight
2
: of or relating to both hearing and sight
