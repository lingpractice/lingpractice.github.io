aboveground

adjective

above·​ground ə-ˈbəv-ˌgrau̇nd 
Synonyms of aboveground
1
: located or occurring on or above the surface of the ground
2
: existing, produced, or published by or within the establishment
aboveground movies
aboveground adverb
