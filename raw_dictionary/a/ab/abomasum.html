abomasum

noun

ab·​oma·​sum ˌa-bō-ˈmā-səm 
plural abomasa ˌa-bō-ˈmā-sə 
: the fourth compartment of the ruminant stomach that follows the omasum and has a true digestive function compare rumen, reticulum
abomasal
ˌa-bō-ˈmā-səl 
adjective

Word History

Etymology

borrowed from New Latin abomāsum, from ab- ab- + omāsum omasum

First Known Use

1678, in the meaning defined above
