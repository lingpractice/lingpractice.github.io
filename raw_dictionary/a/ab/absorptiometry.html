absorptiometry

noun

ab·​sorp·​ti·​om·​e·​try əb-ˌsȯrp-shē-ˈä-mə-trē 
-ˌzȯrp-
: measurement of the amount of radiation absorbed (as by living tissue) especially to determine density compare dual-energy x-ray absorptiometry
