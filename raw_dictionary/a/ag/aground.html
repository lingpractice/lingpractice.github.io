aground

adverb or adjective

ə-ˈgrau̇nd 
Synonyms of aground
1
: on the ground
planes aloft and aground
2
: on or onto the shore or the bottom of a body of water
a ship run aground

Synonyms

  • beached
  • grounded
  • stranded
