assignation

noun

as·​sig·​na·​tion ˌa-sig-ˈnā-shən 
Synonyms of assignation
1
: the act of assigning or the assignment made
2
: an appointment of time and place for a meeting
especially : tryst
returned from an assignation with his mistress —W. B. Yeats
