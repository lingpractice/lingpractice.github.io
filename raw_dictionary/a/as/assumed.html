assumed

adjective

as·​sumed ə-ˈsümd 
Synonyms of assumed
1
: not true or real : deliberately pretended or feigned
an assumed cheerfulness
an assumed air of indifference
: false, fictitious
an assumed name
2
: taken for granted : supposed
the assumed reason for his absence
