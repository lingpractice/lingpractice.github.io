noun

Medical Definition

azine

noun
ˈā-ˌzēn  ˈaz-ˌēn 
1
: any of numerous organic compounds with a nitrogenous 6-membered ring
2
: a compound of the general formula RCH=NN=CHR or R2C=NN=CR2 formed by the action of hydrazine on aldehydes or ketones
