axman

noun

ax·​man ˈaks-mən 
plural axmen
1
: one who wields an ax
2
slang : a guitarist especially in a jazz or rock band
There was nothing pedestrian about the first show of the Pearl Jam tour. Some highlights of the high-octane performance: Lead axman Mike McCready smashed his guitar to pieces …—Michael Goldberg
3
informal : someone who is responsible for making cuts in staff, funding, etc.
a corporate axman
