acetify

verb

ace·​ti·​fy ə-ˈsē-tə-ˌfī  -ˈse- 
acetified; acetifying

transitive verb

: to turn into acetic acid or vinegar
acetification
ə-ˌsē-tə-fə-ˈkā-shən 
-ˌse-
noun

Word History

Etymology

back-formation from acetification, borrowed from New Latin acētificātiōn-, acētificātiō, from Latin acētum "vinegar" + -i- -i- + -ficātiō -fication — more at acetic acid

First Known Use

1789, in the meaning defined above
