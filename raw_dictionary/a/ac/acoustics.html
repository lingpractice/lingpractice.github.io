acoustics

plural noun

acous·​tics ə-ˈkü-stiks 
1
singular in construction : a science that deals with the production, control, transmission, reception, and effects of sound
2
also acoustic ə-ˈkü-stik  : the qualities that determine the ability of an enclosure (such as an auditorium) to reflect sound waves in such a way as to produce distinct hearing
