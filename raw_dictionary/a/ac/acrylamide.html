acrylamide

noun

ac·​ryl·​am·​ide ˌa-krəl-ˈa-ˌmīd  ə-ˈkri-lə- 
: an amide C3H5NO that is derived from acrylic acid, that polymerizes readily, and that is used in the manufacture of synthetic textile fibers
