acyclovir

noun

acy·​clo·​vir (ˌ)ā-ˈsī-klō-ˌvir 
: a cyclic synthetic nucleoside C8H11N5O3 used especially to treat the symptoms of chicken pox, shingles, and the genital form of herpes simplex

Word History
