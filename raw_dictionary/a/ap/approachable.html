approachable

adjective

ap·​proach·​able ə-ˈprō-chə-bəl 
Synonyms of approachable
: capable of being approached : accessible
specifically : easy to meet or deal with
friendly and approachable people
approachability
ə-ˌprō-chə-ˈbi-lə-tē 
noun
