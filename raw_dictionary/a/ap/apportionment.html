apportionment

noun

ap·​por·​tion·​ment ə-ˈpȯr-shən-mənt 
Synonyms of apportionment
: an act or result of apportioning
especially : the apportioning of representatives or taxes among the states according to U.S. law

Synonyms

  • admeasurement
  • allocation
  • allotment
  • disbursement
  • dispensation
  • distribution
  • division
  • issuance
