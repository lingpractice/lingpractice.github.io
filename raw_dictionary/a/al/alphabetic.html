alphabetic

adjective

al·​pha·​bet·​ic ˌal-fə-ˈbe-tik 
variants or alphabetical
ˌal-fə-ˈbe-ti-kəl 
1
usually alphabetical : arranged in the order of the letters of the alphabet
2
: of, relating to, or employing an alphabet
