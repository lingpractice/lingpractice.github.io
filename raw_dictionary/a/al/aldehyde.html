aldehyde

noun

al·​de·​hyde ˈal-də-ˌhīd 
: acetaldehyde
broadly : any of a class of highly reactive organic compounds that are analogous to acetaldehyde and characterized by a carbonyl group attached to a hydrogen atom
aldehydic
ˌal-də-ˈhī-dik 
adjective
