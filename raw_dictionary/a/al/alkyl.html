alkyl

1 of 2

adjective

al·​kyl ˈal-kəl 
: having a monovalent organic group and especially one CnH2n+1 (such as methyl) derived from an alkane (such as methane)

alkyl

2 of 2

noun

: a compound of one or more alkyl groups with a metal
mercury alkyls
