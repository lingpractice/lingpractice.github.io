avenge

verb

ə-ˈvenj 
avenged; avenging
Synonyms of avenge

transitive verb

1
: to take vengeance for or on behalf of
vowed to avenge their murdered father
2
: to exact satisfaction for (a wrong) by punishing the wrongdoer
was determined to avenge the assault
avenger noun

Synonyms

  • redress
  • requite
  • retaliate
  • revenge
  • venge [archaic]
