admeasurement

noun

ad·​mea·​sure·​ment ad-ˈme-zhər-mənt 
-ˈmā-
Synonyms of admeasurement
1
: determination and apportionment of shares
2
: determination or comparison of dimensions
3
: dimensions, size

Synonyms

  • allocation
  • allotment
  • apportionment
  • disbursement
  • dispensation
  • distribution
  • division
  • issuance
See all Synonyms & Antonyms in Thesaurus 

Examples of admeasurement in a Sentence

the admeasurement of the company's profits among its employees is based strictly upon seniority

Word History

Etymology

borrowed from Anglo-French amesurement, from amesurer "to admeasure" + -ment -ment

First Known Use

1527, in the meaning defined at sense 1
