analogical

adjective

an·​a·​log·​i·​cal ˌa-nə-ˈlä-ji-kəl 
variants or less commonly analogic
ˌa-nə-ˈlä-jik 
1
: of, relating to, or based on analogy
2
: expressing or implying analogy
analogically
ˌa-nə-ˈlä-ji-k(ə-)lē 
adverb
