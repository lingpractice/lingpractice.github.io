anabatic

adjective

an·​a·​bat·​ic ˌa-nə-ˈba-tik 
: moving upward : rising
an anabatic wind

Word History

Etymology

Greek anabatos, verbal of anabainein

First Known Use

circa 1918, in the meaning defined above
