aneuploid

adjective

an·​eu·​ploid ˈan-yu̇-ˌploid 
: having or being a chromosome number that is not an exact multiple of the usually haploid number compare euploid
aneuploid noun
aneuploidy
ˈan-yu̇-ˌplȯi-dē
