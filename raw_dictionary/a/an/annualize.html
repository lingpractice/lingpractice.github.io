annualize

verb

an·​nu·​al·​ize ˈan-yə(-wə)-ˌlīz 
-yü-ə-
annualized; annualizing

transitive verb

: to calculate or adjust to reflect a rate based on a full year
quarterly returns yielding at an annualized rate of seven percent
