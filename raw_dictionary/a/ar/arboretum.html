arboretum

noun

ar·​bo·​re·​tum ˌär-bə-ˈrē-təm 
plural arboretums or arboreta ˌär-bə-ˈrē-tə 
Synonyms of arboretum
: a place where trees, shrubs, and herbaceous plants are cultivated for scientific and educational purposes
