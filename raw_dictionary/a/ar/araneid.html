araneid

noun

ara·​ne·​id
ə-ˈrā-nē-əd,
ˌa-rə-ˈnē-,
-ˌid
plural araneids
: a spider of the family Araneidae : orb weaver
Many of them were orb weavers of the family Araneidae. Whatever was fatal for the birds seemed to be highly beneficial for the araneids.—David Quammen, The Song of the Dodo, 1996
broadly : spider sense 1
