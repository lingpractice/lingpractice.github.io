favoring

adjective

fa·​vor·​ing ˈfā-v(ə-)riŋ 
Synonyms of favoring
: providing or tending to provide help in moving forward : being in one's favor : advantageous
a favoring tide
They had flown away in the still June heat like dandelion spores, wafted by a favoring gale.—Christine Carroll
At Cadiz the fleet was delayed by foul winds until 11 May, when it sailed with a favoring northerly.—Samuel Eliot Morison
