flooded

adjective

flood·​ed ˈflə-dəd 
Synonyms of flooded
1
: covered or overfilled with an excess of water or some other liquid
a flooded field
a flooded carburetor/engine
2
: filled, covered, or completely overrun as if by a flood
won't find new customers in a flooded market
