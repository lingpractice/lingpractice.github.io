fluky

adjective

ˈflü-kē 
variants or less commonly flukey
flukier; flukiest
Synonyms of fluky
1
: happening by or depending on chance
2
: being unsteady or uncertain
—used especially of wind

Synonyms

  • fortuitous
  • fortunate
  • happy
  • heaven-sent
  • lucky
  • providential
