flatbread

noun

flat·​bread ˈflat-ˌbred 
1
: a bread (such as focaccia or naan) that has a wide surface and little thickness
2
or flatbrod
ˈflat-ˌbrōd 
[flatbread translation of Norwegian flatbrød; flatbrod modification of Norwegian flatbrød, from flat (from Old Norse flatr) + brød bread, from Old Norse brauth] : a thin, dry wafer made of rye flour dough and used especially among Scandinavian peoples
