flapper

noun

flap·​per ˈfla-pər 
1
a
: something used in flapping or striking
b
: one that flaps
c
: flipper sense 1
2
: a young woman
specifically : a young woman of the period of World War I and the following decade who showed freedom from conventions (as in conduct)
