fiendish

adjective

fiend·​ish ˈfēn-dish 
Synonyms of fiendish
1
: perversely diabolical
took a fiendish pleasure in hurting people
2
: extremely cruel or wicked
3
: excessively bad, unpleasant, or difficult
fiendish weather
fiendishly adverb
fiendishness noun
