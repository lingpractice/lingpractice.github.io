fingerpicking

noun

fin·​ger·​pick·​ing ˈfiŋ-gər-ˌpi-kiŋ 
: a method of playing a stringed instrument (such as a guitar) with the thumb and tips of the fingers rather than with a pick
fingerpick
ˈfiŋ-gər-ˌpik
