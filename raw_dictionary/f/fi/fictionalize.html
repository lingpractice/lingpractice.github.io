fictionalize

verb

fic·​tion·​al·​ize ˈfik-shnə-ˌlīz 
-shə-nə-ˌlīz
fictionalized; fictionalizing

transitive verb

: to make into or treat in the manner of fiction
fictionalize a biography
fictionalization
ˌfik-shnə-lə-ˈzā-shən 
-shə-nə-lə-
noun
