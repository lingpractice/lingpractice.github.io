filovirus

noun

fi·​lo·​vi·​rus ˈfī-lō-ˌvī-rəs 
: any of a family (Filoviridae) of single-stranded chiefly filamentous RNA viruses that infect vertebrates and include the Marburg virus and the Ebola viruses
