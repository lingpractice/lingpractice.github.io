fiddlestick

noun

fid·​dle·​stick ˈfi-dᵊl-ˌstik 
1
: a violin bow
2
a
: something of little value : trifle
didn't care a fiddlestick for that
b
fiddlesticks ˈfi-dᵊl-ˌstiks  plural : nonsense
—used as an interjection
