Synonyms of ferment

intransitive verb

1
: to undergo fermentation

2
: to be in a state of agitation or intense activity

transitive verb

1
: to cause to undergo fermentation
Yeast ferments the sugar in the juice.
2
: to work up (as into a state of agitation) : foment
