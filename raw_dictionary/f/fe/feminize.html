feminize

verb

fem·​i·​nize ˈfe-mə-ˌnīz 
feminized; feminizing

transitive verb

1
: to give a feminine quality to
2
: to cause (a male or castrate) to take on feminine characters (as by implantation of ovaries or administration of estrogens)
feminization
ˌfe-mə-nə-ˈzā-shən 
noun
