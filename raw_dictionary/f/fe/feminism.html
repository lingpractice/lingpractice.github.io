feminism

noun

fem·​i·​nism ˈfe-mə-ˌni-zəm 
: belief in and advocacy of the political, economic, and social equality of the sexes expressed especially through organized activity on behalf of women's rights and interests
feministic
ˌfe-mə-ˈni-stik 
adjective
