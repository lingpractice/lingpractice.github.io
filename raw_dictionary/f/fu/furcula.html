furcula

noun

fur·​cu·​la ˈfər-kyə-lə 
plural furculae ˈfər-kyə-ˌlē 
-ˌlī
: a forked process or part: such as
a
: wishbone
b
: the forked leaping appendage arising from the fourth abdominal segment of a springtail
