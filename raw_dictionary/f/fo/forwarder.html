forwarder

noun

for·​ward·​er ˈfȯr-wər-dər 
 also  ˈfō-,
 or  ˈfȯ-,
 Southern also  ˈfär-
: one that forwards
especially : an agent who performs services (such as receiving, transshipping, or delivering) designed to move goods to their destination
