forecastle

noun

fore·​cas·​tle ˈfōk-səl 
ˈfȯr-ˌka-səl
variants or fo'c'sle
ˈfōk-səl 
1
: the forward part of the upper deck of a ship
2
: the crew's quarters usually in a ship's bow
