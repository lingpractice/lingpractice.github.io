fortepiano

1 of 2

noun

for·​te·​pia·​no ˌfȯr-ˌtā-pē-ˈa-(ˌ)nō 
also
-ˈä-(ˌ)nō 
: an early form of the piano originating in the 18th and early 19th centuries and having a smaller range and softer timbre than a modern piano
