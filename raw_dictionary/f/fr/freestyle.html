freestyle

noun

free·​style ˈfrē-ˌstī(-ə)l 
often attributive
1
: a competition in which the contestant is given more latitude than in related events
especially : a swimming competition in which the swimmer may use any stroke
2
: crawl sense 2
freestyler noun
