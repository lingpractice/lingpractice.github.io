freshet

noun

fresh·​et ˈfre-shət 
Synonyms of freshet
1
archaic : stream sense 1
2
a
: a great rise or overflowing of a stream caused by heavy rains or melted snow
b
: a swelling quantity : influx
summer brings a freshet of tourists
