yielding

adjective

yield·​ing ˈyēl-diŋ 
Synonyms of yielding
1
: productive
a high-yielding wheat
2
: lacking rigidity or stiffness : flexible
3
: disposed to submit or comply
a docile and yielding temperament

Synonyms

  • acquiescent
  • nonresistant
  • passive
  • resigned
  • tolerant
