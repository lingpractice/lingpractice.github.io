yuppie

noun

yup·​pie ˈyə-pē 
often capitalized
: a young college-educated adult who is employed in a well-paying profession and who lives and works in or near a large city
yuppiedom
ˈyə-pē-dəm 
adjective
yuppieish
ˈyə-pē-ish 
adjective
