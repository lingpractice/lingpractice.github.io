yarrow

noun

yar·​row ˈyer-(ˌ)ō 
ˈya-(ˌ)rō
: a widely naturalized strong-scented Eurasian composite herb (Achillea millefolium) with finely dissected leaves and small usually white corymbose flowers
also : any of several congeneric plants
