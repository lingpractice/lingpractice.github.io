equipage

noun

eq·​ui·​page ˈe-kwə-pij 
Synonyms of equipage
1
a
: material or articles used in equipment : outfit
b archaic
(1)
: a set of small articles (as for table service)
(2)
: etui
c
: trappings
2
archaic : retinue
3
: a horse-drawn carriage with its servants
also : such a carriage alone
