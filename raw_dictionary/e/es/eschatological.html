eschatological

adjective

es·​cha·​to·​log·​i·​cal (ˌ)e-ˌska-tə-ˈlä-ji-kəl 
ˌe-skə-
1
: of or relating to eschatology or an eschatology
2
: of or relating to the end of the world or the events associated with it in eschatology
