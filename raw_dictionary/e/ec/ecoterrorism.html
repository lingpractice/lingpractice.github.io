ecoterrorism

noun

eco·​ter·​ror·​ism ˌē-kō-ˈter-ər-i-zəm 
ˌe-kō-
1
: sabotage intended to hinder activities that are considered damaging to the environment
2
: political terrorism intended to damage an enemy's natural environment
