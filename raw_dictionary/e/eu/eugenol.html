eugenol

noun

eu·​ge·​nol ˈyü-jə-ˌnȯl  -ˌnōl 
: a colorless aromatic liquid phenol C10H12O2 found especially in clove oil and used commercially in flavors and perfumes and in dentistry as an analgesic
