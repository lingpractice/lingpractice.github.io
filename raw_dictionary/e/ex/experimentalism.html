experimentalism

noun

ex·​per·​i·​men·​tal·​ism ik-ˌsper-ə-ˈmen-tə-ˌli-zəm 
 also  -ˌspir-
: reliance on or advocacy of experimental or empirical principles and procedures
specifically : instrumentalism
