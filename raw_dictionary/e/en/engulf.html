engulf

verb

en·​gulf in-ˈgəlf 
en-
engulfed; engulfing; engulfs
Synonyms of engulf

transitive verb

1
: to flow over and enclose : overwhelm
the mounting seas threatened to engulf the island
2
: to take in (food) by or as if by flowing over and enclosing
