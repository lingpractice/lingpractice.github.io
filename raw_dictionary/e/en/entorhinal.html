entorhinal

adjective

en·​to·​rhi·​nal ˌen-tə-ˈrī-nᵊl 
: of, relating to, or being the part of the cerebral cortex in the medial temporal lobe that serves as the main cortical input to the hippocampus

Word History

Etymology
