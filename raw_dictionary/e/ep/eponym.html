eponym

noun

ep·​onym ˈe-pə-ˌnim 
1
: one for whom or which something is or is believed to be named
2
: a name (as of a drug or a disease) based on or derived from an eponym
eponymic
ˌe-pə-ˈni-mik 
adjective
