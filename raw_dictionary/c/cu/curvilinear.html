curvilinear

adjective

cur·​vi·​lin·​ear ˌkər-və-ˈli-nē-ər 
1
: consisting of or bounded by curved lines : represented by a curved line
2
: marked by flowing tracery
curvilinear Gothic
curvilinearity
ˌkər-və-ˌli-nē-ˈer-ə-tē
