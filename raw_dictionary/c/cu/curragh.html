curragh

noun

cur·​ragh ˈkə-rə  -rəḵ 
variants or currach
Synonyms of curragh
: a usually large coracle used especially on the west coast of Ireland

Word History

Etymology

Middle English currok, from Irish currach, curach

First Known Use

15th century, in the meaning defined above
