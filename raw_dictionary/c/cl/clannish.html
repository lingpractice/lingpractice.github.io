clannish

adjective

clan·​nish ˈkla-nish 
Synonyms of clannish
1
: of or relating to a clan
clannish traditions
2
: tending to associate only with a select group of similar background or status
a clannish community
clannishly adverb
clannishness noun

Synonyms

  • cliquey
  • cliquish
  • close-knit
