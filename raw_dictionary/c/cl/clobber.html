clobber

2 of 2

verb

clobbered; clobbering ˈklä-b(ə-)riŋ 

transitive verb

1
: to pound mercilessly
also : to hit with force
clobber a home run
2
a
: to defeat overwhelmingly
b
: to have a strongly negative impact on
businesses clobbered by the recession
c
: to criticize harshly
