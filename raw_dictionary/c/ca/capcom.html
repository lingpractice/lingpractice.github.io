capcom

noun

cap·​com ˈkap-ˌkäm 
variants or Capcom or less commonly CapCom or CAPCOM
plural capcoms or Capcoms also CapComs or CAPCOMs
: a member of the ground support staff for a crewed space mission who is responsible for communicating via radio with the crew during the mission
All the astronauts chosen as capcoms automatically become members of a flight's support team, attending training sessions and computer-simulated practice flights along with the actual crew.—Susan Okie
