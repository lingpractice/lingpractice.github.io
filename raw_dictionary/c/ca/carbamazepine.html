carbamazepine

noun

car·​ba·​maz·​e·​pine ˌkär-bə-ˈma-zə-ˌpēn 
: a tricyclic anticonvulsant and analgesic C15H12N2O used in the treatment of trigeminal neuralgia and epilepsy
