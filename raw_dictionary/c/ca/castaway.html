castaway

adjective

cast·​away ˈkast-ə-ˌwā 
Synonyms of castaway
1
: thrown away : rejected
2
a
: cast adrift or ashore as a survivor of a shipwreck
b
: thrown out or left without friends or resources
castaway noun
