cannibalism

noun

can·​ni·​bal·​ism ˈka-nə-bə-ˌli-zəm 
1
: the usually ritualistic eating of human flesh by a human being
2
: the eating of the flesh of an animal by another animal of the same kind
3
: an act of cannibalizing something
cannibalistic
ˌka-nə-bə-ˈli-stik 
adjective
