carrousel

noun

car·​rou·​sel

less common spelling of carousel

1
: a tournament or exhibition in which horsemen execute evolutions
2
a
: merry-go-round
b
: a circular conveyor
the luggage carousel at the airport
c
: a revolving case or tray used for storage or display
