calcite

noun

cal·​cite ˈkal-ˌsīt 
: a mineral CaCO3 consisting of calcium carbonate crystallized in hexagonal form and including common limestone, chalk, and marble compare aragonite
calcitic
kal-ˈsi-tik 
adjective
