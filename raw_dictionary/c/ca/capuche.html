capuche

noun

ca·​puche kə-ˈpüch  -ˈpüsh 
Synonyms of capuche
: hood
especially : the cowl of a Capuchin friar

Word History

Etymology

Middle French, from Italian cappuccio, from cappa cloak, from Late Latin

First Known Use

circa 1600, in the meaning defined above
