cigarette

noun

cig·​a·​rette ˌsi-gə-ˈret 
ˈsi-gə-ˌret
variants or less commonly cigaret
: a slender roll of cut tobacco enclosed in paper and meant to be smoked
also : a similar roll of another substance (such as marijuana)
