cinch

2 of 2

verb

cinched; cinching; cinches

transitive verb

1
a
: to put a cinch on
cinch a horse
b
: to fasten (something, such as a belt or strap) tightly
He cinched his belt tight.
2
: to make certain : assure
the goal that cinched the victory

intransitive verb

: to tighten the cinch
—often used with up
