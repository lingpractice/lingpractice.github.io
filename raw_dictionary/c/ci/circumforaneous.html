circumforaneous

adjective

cir·​cum·​fo·​ra·​ne·​ous
¦sərkəmfə¦rānēəs,
sər¦k-
: going about from market to market : wandering from place to place
a circumforaneous jester

Word History

Etymology

Latin circumforaneus, from circum- + -foraneus (from forum market place)
