ceftriaxone

noun

cef·​tri·​ax·​one ˌsef-ˌtrī-ˈak-ˌsōn 
: a broad-spectrum semisynthetic cephalosporin antibiotic C18H18N8O7S3 administered parenterally in the form of its sodium salt
