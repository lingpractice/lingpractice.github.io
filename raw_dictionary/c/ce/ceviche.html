ceviche

noun

ce·​vi·​che sə-ˈvē-(ˌ)chā 
-chē
variants or seviche
: a dish made of raw fish marinated in lime or lemon juice often with oil, onions, peppers, and seasonings and sometimes served as a snack or appetizer
