conceivably

adverb

con·​ceiv·​ably kən-ˈsē-və-blē 
Synonyms of conceivably
1
: in a conceivable manner
2
: it may be conceived : possibly
we could conceivably finish next week

Synonyms

  • maybe
  • mayhap
  • perchance
  • perhaps
  • possibly
