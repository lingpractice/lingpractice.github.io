cornrow

noun

corn·​row ˈkȯrn-ˌrō 
Synonyms of cornrow
1
: a section of hair which is braided usually flat to the scalp
2
: a hairstyle in which the hair is divided into cornrow sections arranged in rows
cornrow verb
