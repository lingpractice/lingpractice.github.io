coloristic

adjective

col·​or·​is·​tic ˌkə-lə-ˈri-stik 
1
: of or relating to color or coloring
2
: of or relating to timbre in music
coloristically
ˌkə-lə-ˈri-sti-k(ə-)lē 
adverb
