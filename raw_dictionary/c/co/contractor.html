contractor

noun

con·​trac·​tor
sense 1 is usually
ˈkän-ˌtrak-tər 
sense 2 is usually
kən-ˈtrak- 
1
: one that contracts or is party to a contract: such as
a
: one that contracts to perform work or provide supplies
b
: one that contracts to erect buildings
2
: something (such as a muscle) that contracts or shortens
