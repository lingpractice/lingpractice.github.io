counterdemonstration

noun

coun·​ter·​dem·​on·​stra·​tion ˌkau̇n-tər-ˌde-mən-ˈstrā-shən 
variants or counter-demonstration
plural counterdemonstrations or counter-demonstrations
Synonyms of counterdemonstration
: a demonstration (see demonstration sense 4) occurring in opposition to another demonstration
… CBS turned its attention to a demonstration in Manhattan against the government's Vietnam policy, with a counterdemonstration of pickets screaming that the protesters were disloyal.—William E. Leuchtenburg
