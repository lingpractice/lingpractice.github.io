comma

noun

com·​ma ˈkä-mə 
Synonyms of comma
1
: a punctuation mark, used especially as a mark of separation within the sentence
2
: pause, interval
3
: any of several nymphalid butterflies (genus Polygonia) with a silvery comma-shaped mark on the underside of the hind wings
