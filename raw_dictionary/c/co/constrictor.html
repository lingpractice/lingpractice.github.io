constrictor

noun

con·​stric·​tor kən-ˈstrik-tər 
Synonyms of constrictor
1
: a muscle that contracts a cavity or orifice or compresses an organ
2
: a snake (such as a boa constrictor) that coils around and compresses prey
3
: one that constricts
