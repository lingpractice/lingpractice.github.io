confectionery

noun

con·​fec·​tion·​ery kən-ˈfek-shə-ˌner-ē 
plural confectioneries
Synonyms of confectionery
1
: the confectioner's art or business
2
: sweet foods (such as candy or pastry)
3
: a confectioner's shop
