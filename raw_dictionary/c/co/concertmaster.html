concertmaster

noun

con·​cert·​mas·​ter ˈkän(t)-sərt-ˌma-stər 
variants or concertmeister
ˈkän(t)-sərt-ˌmī-stər 
: the leader of the first violins of an orchestra and by custom usually the assistant to the conductor
