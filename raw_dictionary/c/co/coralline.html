coralline

1 of 2

noun

cor·​al·​line ˈkȯr-ə-ˌlīn 
ˈkär-
1
: a coralline red alga
2
: a bryozoan or hydroid that resembles a coral

coralline

2 of 2

adjective

1
: of, relating to, or resembling coral
2
: of, relating to, or being any of a family (Corallinaceae) of calcareous red algae
