colorific

adjective

col·​or·​if·​ic ˌkə-lə-ˈri-fik 
: capable of communicating color

Word History

Etymology

borrowed from New Latin colorificus, from Latin color color entry 1 + -i- -i- + -ficus -fic

First Known Use

1676, in the meaning defined above
