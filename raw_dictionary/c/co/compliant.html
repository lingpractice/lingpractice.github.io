compliant

adjective

com·​pli·​ant kəm-ˈplī-ənt 
Synonyms of compliant
1
: ready or disposed to comply : submissive
a corrupt regime aided by a compliant press
was compliant and eager to please
2
: conforming to requirements
compliant software
compliantly adverb
