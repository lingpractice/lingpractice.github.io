couplet

noun

cou·​plet ˈkə-plət 
Synonyms of couplet
1
: two successive lines of verse forming a unit marked usually by rhythmic correspondence, rhyme, or the inclusion of a self-contained utterance : distich
She ended her poem with a rhyming couplet.
2
: couple
