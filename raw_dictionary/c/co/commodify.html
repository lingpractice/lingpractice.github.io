commodify

verb

com·​mod·​i·​fy kə-ˈmä-də-ˌfī 
commodified; commodifying
Synonyms of commodify

transitive verb

: to turn (something, such as an intrinsic value or a work of art) into a commodity
attempts to commodify the water supply
