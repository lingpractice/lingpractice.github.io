codirect

verb

co·​di·​rect ˌkō-də-ˈrekt 
-dī-
variants or co-direct
codirected or co-directed; codirecting or co-directing
Synonyms of codirect

transitive + intransitive

: to share the duties of directing (something) with another or others : to direct (something) jointly
codirect a film
co-directing a seminar on online advertising
A couple of years ago I inherited a job codirecting a program that integrates children's books into pediatric practice.—Perri Klass
Carlos and Christie Vela not only co-star but co-direct, working in tandem onstage and off.—Lindsey Wilson
