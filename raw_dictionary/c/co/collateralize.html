collateralize

verb

col·​lat·​er·​al·​ize kə-ˈla-t(ə-)rə-ˌlīz 
collateralized; collateralizing

transitive verb

1
: to make (a loan) secure with collateral
2
: to use (something, such as securities) for collateral
