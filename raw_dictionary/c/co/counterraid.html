counterraid

noun

coun·​ter·​raid ˈkau̇n-tər-ˌrād 
variants or counter-raid
plural counterraids or counter-raids
: a raid carried out in response to or retaliation for another raid
… raids and counterraids had claimed several thousand lives …—Brian DeLay
