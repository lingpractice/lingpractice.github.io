chateaubriand

noun

cha·​teau·​bri·​and (ˌ)sha-ˌtō-brē-ˈän(d)  (ˌ)sha-ˌtō-brē-ˈäⁿ 
often capitalized
: a large tenderloin steak usually grilled or broiled and served with a sauce (such as béarnaise)
