chinquapin

noun

chin·​qua·​pin ˈchiŋ-ki-ˌpin 
variants or chinkapin
1
: the edible nut of a chinquapin
2
: any of several trees (genera Castanea and Castanopsis)
especially : a dwarf chestnut (Castanea pumila) of the U.S.
