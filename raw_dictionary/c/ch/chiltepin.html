chiltepin

noun

chil·​te·​pin ˈchil-tə-ˌpēn 
variants or less commonly chiltepine
: a small red oblong wild chili pepper of marked pungency that is produced by a capsicum (Capsicum annuum glabriusculum synonym C. annuum aviculare) occurring from northern South America to northern Mexico and the southwestern U.S.
also : this plant
