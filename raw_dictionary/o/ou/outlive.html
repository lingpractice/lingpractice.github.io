outlive

verb

out·​live ˌau̇t-ˈliv 
outlived; outliving; outlives
Synonyms of outlive

transitive verb

1
: to live beyond or longer than
outlived most of his friends
outlive its usefulness
2
: to survive the effects of
universities … outlive many political and social changes—J. B. Conant

Synonyms

  • outlast
  • outwear
