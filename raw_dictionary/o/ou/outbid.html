outbid

verb

out·​bid ˌau̇t-ˈbid 
outbid; outbidden ˌau̇t-ˈbi-dᵊn  ; outbidding

transitive verb

: to make a higher bid than : to offer more than
… when employers clamor to outbid each other for the services of an engineering elite …—Randall E. Stross
