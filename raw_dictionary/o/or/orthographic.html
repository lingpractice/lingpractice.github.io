orthographic

adjective

or·​tho·​graph·​ic ˌȯr-thə-ˈgra-fik 
variants or less commonly orthographical
ˌȯr-thə-ˈgra-fi-kəl 
1
: of, relating to, being, or prepared by orthographic projection
an orthographic map
2
a
: of or relating to orthography
b
: correct in spelling
orthographically
ˌȯr-thə-ˈgra-fi-k(ə-)lē 
adverb
