organized

adjective

or·​ga·​nized ˈȯr-gə-ˌnīzd 
Synonyms of organized
1
: having a formal organization to coordinate and carry out activities
organized baseball
organized crime
2
: affiliated by membership in an organization (such as a union)
organized steelworkers
