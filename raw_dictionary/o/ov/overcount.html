overcount

verb

over·​count ˌō-vər-ˈkau̇nt 
overcounted; overcounting

transitive verb

: to count more of (people or things) than is accurate
overcounted the votes
overcounted the number of students enrolled
overcount
ˈō-vər-ˌkau̇nt 
noun
plural overcounts
an overcount in the census
