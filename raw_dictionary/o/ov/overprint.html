verb

over·​print ˌō-vər-ˈprint 
overprinted; overprinting; overprints

transitive verb

overprint

2 of 2

noun

over·​print ˈō-vər-ˌprint 
: something added by or as if by overprinting
especially : a printed marking added to a postage or revenue stamp especially to alter the original or to commemorate a special event
