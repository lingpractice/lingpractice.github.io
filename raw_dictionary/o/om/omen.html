omen

noun

ˈō-mən 
Synonyms of omen
: an occurrence or phenomenon (see phenomenon sense 1) believed to portend a future event : augury
The dark clouds were considered a bad omen.

Synonyms

  • augury
  • auspice
  • boding

  • portent
