onshore

adjective

on·​shore ˈȯn-ˌshȯr 
ˈän-
1
: coming or moving from the water toward or onto the shore
an onshore wind
2
a
: situated on or near the shore as distinguished from being in deep or open water
b
: situated on land
3
: domestic sense 2
onshore markets

ˈȯn-ˈshȯr
