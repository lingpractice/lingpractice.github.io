unhook

verb

un·​hook ˌən-ˈhu̇k 
unhooked; unhooking; unhooks
Synonyms of unhook

transitive verb

1
: to remove from a hook
2
: to unfasten by disengaging a hook
3
: to free from a habit or dependency
