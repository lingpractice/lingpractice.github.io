unrated

adjective

un·​rat·​ed ˌən-ˈrā-təd 
: not rated
unrated bonds
unrated teams
especially : not assigned a certification mark (such as G, NC-17, PG, PG-13, or R)
The movie is unrated.
the unrated version of a film
