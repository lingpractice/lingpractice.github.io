unscheduled

adjective

un·​sched·​uled ˌən-ˈske-(ˌ)jüld 
-jəld,
 Canadian also  -ˈshe-,
 British usually  -ˈshe-(ˌ)dyüld
: not appointed, assigned, or designated for a fixed time : not scheduled
made an unscheduled stop
unscheduled visits
