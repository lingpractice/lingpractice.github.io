unfix

verb

un·​fix ˌən-ˈfiks 
unfixed; unfixing; unfixes
Synonyms of unfix

transitive verb

1
: to loosen from a fastening : detach, disengage
2
: to make unstable : unsettle

Word History

First Known Use

1597, in the meaning defined at sense 1
