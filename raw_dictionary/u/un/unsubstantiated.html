unsubstantiated

adjective

un·​sub·​stan·​ti·​at·​ed ˌən-səb-ˈstan(t)-shē-ˌā-təd 
Synonyms of unsubstantiated
: not proven to be true : not substantiated
an unsubstantiated rumor/report
a plausible but unsubstantiated theory
