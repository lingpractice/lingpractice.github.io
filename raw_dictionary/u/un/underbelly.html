underbelly

noun

un·​der·​bel·​ly ˈən-dər-ˌbe-lē 
Synonyms of underbelly
1
: a vulnerable area
also : a corrupt or sordid part
probing the underbelly of the entertainment industry
2
: the underside of a body or mass

  • demiworld
