unrefined

adjective

un·​re·​fined ˌən-ri-ˈfīnd 
Synonyms of unrefined
: not refined: such as
a
: lacking moral or social cultivation : coarse, uncouth
unrefined tastes and manners
b
: not separated from dross, impurity, or unwanted matter
unrefined oil/ore

Synonyms

  • native

  • rude

  • untreated
