unsalvageable

adjective

un·​sal·​vage·​able ˌən-ˈsal-vi-jə-bəl 
: not capable of being salvaged : not salvageable
a house in unsalvageable condition
an unsalvageable relationship
unsalvageably
ˌən-ˈsal-vi-jə-blē 
adverb
a house in unsalvageably bad condition
