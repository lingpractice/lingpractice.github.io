unthinking

adjective

un·​think·​ing ˌən-ˈthiŋ-kiŋ 
Synonyms of unthinking
1
: not taking thought : heedless, unmindful
the unthinking onlookers
2
: not indicating thought or reflection
an unthinking decision
3
: not having the power of thought
unthinkingly
ˌən-ˈthiŋ-kiŋ-lē 
adverb
