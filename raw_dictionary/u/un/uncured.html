uncured

adjective

un·​cured ˌən-ˈkyu̇rd 
: not cured: such as
a
: not subjected to a preservative process
uncured meats/cheeses
b
: not restored to health
an uncured patient
c
: not remedied or eliminated
a disease that has gone uncured
