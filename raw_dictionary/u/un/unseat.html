unseat

verb

un·​seat ˌən-ˈsēt 
unseated; unseating; unseats
Synonyms of unseat

transitive verb

1
: to dislodge from one's seat especially on horseback
2
: to remove from a place or position
especially : to remove from political office

Synonyms
