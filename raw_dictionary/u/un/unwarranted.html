unwarranted

adjective

un·​war·​rant·​ed ˌən-ˈwȯr-ən-təd 
-ˈwär-
Synonyms of unwarranted
: lacking adequate or official support : not warranted : unjustified
unwarranted fears
an unwarranted intrusion
Their criticism is unwarranted.
