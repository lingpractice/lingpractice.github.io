unsavory

adjective

un·​sa·​vory ˌən-ˈsā-və-rē 
-ˈsāv-rē
Synonyms of unsavory
1
: insipid, tasteless
2
a
: unpleasant to taste or smell
b
: disagreeable, distasteful

especially : morally offensive
unsavory business practices
