uncelebrated

adjective

un·​cel·​e·​brat·​ed ˌən--ˈse-lə-ˌbrā-təd 
Synonyms of uncelebrated
1
: not formally honored or commemorated
2
: not famous : obscure
solid but uncelebrated players
