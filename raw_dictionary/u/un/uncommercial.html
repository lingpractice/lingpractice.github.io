uncommercial

adjective

un·​com·​mer·​cial ˌən-kə-ˈmər-shəl 
Synonyms of uncommercial
1
: not engaged in or related to commerce
2
: not based on commercial principles
3
: not likely to result in financial success
an uncommercial book
