unroll

verb

un·​roll ˌən-ˈrōl 
unrolled; unrolling; unrolls
Synonyms of unroll

transitive verb

1
: to unwind a roll of : open out : uncoil
2
: to spread out like a scroll for reading or inspection : unfold, reveal

intransitive verb

: to be unrolled : unwind
