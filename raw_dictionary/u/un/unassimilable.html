unassimilable

adjective

un·​as·​sim·​i·​la·​ble ˌən-ə-ˈsi-mə-lə-bəl 
: not able to be taken in or absorbed : not capable of being assimilated
… misfits … and revolutionaries deemed unassimilable by mainstream culture—James Traub
an unassimilable ideology
unassimilably
ˌən-ə-ˈsi-mə-lə-blē 
adverb
