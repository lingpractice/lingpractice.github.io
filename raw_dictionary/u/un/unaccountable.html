unaccountable

adjective

un·​ac·​count·​able ˌən-ə-ˈkau̇n-tə-bəl 
Synonyms of unaccountable
1
: not to be accounted for : inexplicable, strange
2
: not to be called to account : not responsible
unaccountability
ˌən-ə-ˌkau̇n-tə-ˈbi-lə-tē 
noun
