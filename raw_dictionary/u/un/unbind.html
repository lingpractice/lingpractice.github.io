unbind

verb

un·​bind ˌən-ˈbīnd 
unbound ˌən-ˈbau̇nd  ; unbinding
Synonyms of unbind

transitive verb

1
: to remove a band from : free from fastenings : untie, unfasten
2
: to set free : release

Synonyms

  • unfasten
  • unlash
  • untie
