unprotected

adjective

un·​pro·​tect·​ed ˌən-prə-ˈtek-təd 
Synonyms of unprotected
1
: lacking protection or defense
unprotected troops
skin unprotected from the sun's rays
2
: performed without the use of birth control to prevent pregnancy
also : performed without the use of a condom to prevent spread of sexually transmitted disease
unprotected sex

Synonyms

  • defenseless
  • exposed
  • helpless
  • susceptible
  • undefended
  • unguarded
  • unresistant
  • vulnerable
