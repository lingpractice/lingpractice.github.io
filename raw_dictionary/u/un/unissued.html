unissued

adjective

un·​is·​sued ˌən-ˈi-(ˌ)shüd 
 chiefly Southern  -ˈi-shəd,
 chiefly British  -ˈis-(ˌ)yüd
: not put forth, distributed, or made available : not issued
a previously unissued recording
unissued stock
