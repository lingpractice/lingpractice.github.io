ultracivilized

adjective

ul·​tra·​civ·​i·​lized ˌəl-trə-ˈsi-və-ˌlīzd 
variants or ultra-civilized
: highly civilized : supercivilized: such as
a
: advanced to a very high level of cultural or technological development
an ultracivilized city/society
b
: characterized by a very high degree of taste, refinement, or restraint
ultracivilized dining
her ultracivilized speech/manner
… the ultra-civilized, silver-haired British broadcaster best known to American audiences as the host of "Masterpiece Theatre" …—Adam Bernstein
… an ultra-civilized way to commune with nature without sacrificing 1,000-thread-count sheets, hot showers and housekeeping.—Josh Sens
