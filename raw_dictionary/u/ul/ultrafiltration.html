ultrafiltration

noun

ul·​tra·​fil·​tra·​tion ˌəl-trə-fil-ˈtrā-shən 
: filtration through a medium (such as a semipermeable capillary wall) which allows small molecules (as of water) to pass but holds back larger ones (as of protein)
ultrafiltrate
ˌəl-trə-ˈfil-ˌtrāt 
noun
