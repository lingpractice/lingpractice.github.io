ulna

noun

ul·​na ˈəl-nə 
plural ulnae ˈəl-nē  or ulnas
: the bone on the little-finger side of the human forearm
also : a corresponding part of the forelimb of vertebrates above fishes
ulnar
ˈəl-nər 
-ˌnär
adjective
