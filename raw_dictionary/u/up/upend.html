upend

verb

up·​end ˌəp-ˈend 
upended; upending; upends
Synonyms of upend

transitive verb

1
: to set or stand on end
also : overturn sense 1
2
a
: to affect to the point of being upset or flurried
a … literary shocker, designed to upend the credulous matrons—Wolcott Gibbs
b
: defeat, beat

intransitive verb

: to rise on an end
