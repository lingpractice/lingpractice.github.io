uracil

noun

ura·​cil ˈyu̇r-ə-ˌsil 
-səl
: a pyrimidine base C4H4N2O2 that is one of the four bases coding genetic information in the polynucleotide chain of RNA compare adenine, cytosine, guanine, thymine
