gillnet

1 of 2

verb

gill·​net ˈgil-ˌnet 
gillnetted; gillnetting; gillnets

transitive verb

: to catch (fish) with a gill net

gill net

2 of 2

noun

: a flat net suspended vertically in the water with meshes that allow the head of a fish to pass but entangle the gills upon withdrawal
