goodness

noun

good·​ness ˈgu̇d-nəs 
Synonyms of goodness
1
: the quality or state of being good
2
—used interjectionally or in phrases especially to express mild surprise or shock
oh, my goodness!
goodness knows
3
: the nutritious, flavorful, or beneficial part of something
