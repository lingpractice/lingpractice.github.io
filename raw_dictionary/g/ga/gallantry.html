gallantry

noun

gal·​lant·​ry ˈga-lən-trē 
plural gallantries
Synonyms of gallantry
1
archaic : gallant appearance
2
a
: an act of marked courtesy
b
: courteous attention to a lady
c
: amorous attention or pursuit
3
: spirited and conspicuous bravery
