geoscience

noun

geo·​sci·​ence ˌjē-ō-ˈsī-ən(t)s 
1
: the sciences (such as geology, geophysics, and geochemistry) dealing with the earth
2
: any of the geosciences compare earth science
geoscientist
ˌjē-ō-ˈsī-ən-tist 
noun
