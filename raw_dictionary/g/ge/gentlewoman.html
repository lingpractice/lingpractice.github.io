gentlewoman

noun

gen·​tle·​wom·​an ˈjen-tᵊl-ˌwu̇-mən 
Synonyms of gentlewoman
1
a
: a woman of noble or gentle birth
b
: a woman who is an attendant upon a lady of rank
2
: a woman of refined manners or good breeding : lady
