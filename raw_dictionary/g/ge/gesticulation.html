gesticulation

noun

ges·​tic·​u·​la·​tion je-ˌsti-kyə-ˈlā-shən 
Synonyms of gesticulation
1
: the act of making gestures
2
: gesture
especially : an expressive gesture made in showing strong feeling or in enforcing an argument

Synonyms

  • gesture
  • mime
  • pantomime
  • sign
  • signal
