gesso

noun

ges·​so ˈje-(ˌ)sō 
plural gessoes
1
: plaster of paris or gypsum prepared with glue for use in painting or making bas-reliefs
2
: a paste prepared by mixing whiting with size or glue and spread upon a surface to fit it for painting or gilding
gessoed
ˈje-(ˌ)sōd 
adjective
