redial

1 of 3

noun

re·​dial ˈrē-ˌdī(-ə)l 
rē-ˈdī(-ə)l
: a function on a telephone that automatically repeats the dialing of the last number called
also : a button that invokes this function

redial

2 of 3

verb

redialed; redialing; redials

transitive + intransitive

: to dial a phone number again
redial a busy number
Some phones can be set to automatically redial after a short delay.

redial

3 of 3

adjective

re·​di·​al ˈrē-dē-əl 
: of, relating to, or being the redia or rediae of a digenetic trematode
redial larvae
