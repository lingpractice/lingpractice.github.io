rechromatography

noun

re·​chro·​ma·​tog·​ra·​phy (ˌ)rē-ˌkrō-mə-ˈtä-grə-fē 
: a repeated process or instance of chromatography
rechromatography of two partially purified fractions
rechromatograph
(ˌ)rē-krō-ˈma-tə-ˌgraf 
-krə-
transitive verb
rechromatographed; rechromatographing; rechromatographs
rechromatographed the eluate
