revive

verb

re·​vive ri-ˈvīv 
revived; reviving
Synonyms of revive

intransitive verb

: to return to consciousness or life : become active or flourishing again

transitive verb

1
: to restore to consciousness or life
2
: to restore from a depressed, inactive, or unused state : bring back
3
: to renew in the mind or memory
