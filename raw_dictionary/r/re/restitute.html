restitute

verb

res·​ti·​tute ˈre-stə-ˌtüt 
-ˌtyüt
restituted; restituting
Synonyms of restitute

transitive verb

1
: to restore to a former state or position
2
: give back
especially : refund

intransitive verb

: to undergo restitution
