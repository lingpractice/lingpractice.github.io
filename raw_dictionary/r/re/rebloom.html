rebloom

verb

re·​bloom (ˌ)rē-ˈblüm 
rebloomed; reblooming; reblooms

intransitive verb

: to bloom again
especially : to bloom again in the same growing season
Certain varieties of iris will rebloom in late fall.
a reblooming daylily
rebloomer noun
plural rebloomers
These roses are dependable rebloomers.
