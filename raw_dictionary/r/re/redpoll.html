redpoll

1 of 2

noun (1)

red·​poll ˈred-ˌpōl 
: either of two small finches (genus Carduelis synonym Acanthis of the family Fringillidae) having brownish streaked plumage and a red or rosy crown
especially : one (C. flammea) found in northern regions of both the New and Old World

red poll

2 of 2

noun (2)

often capitalized R&P
: any of a breed of large hornless red beef cattle of English origin
