reseed

verb

re·​seed (ˌ)rē-ˈsēd 
reseeded; reseeding; reseeds
Synonyms of reseed

transitive verb

1
: to sow seed on again or anew
2
: to maintain (itself) by self-sown seed

intransitive verb

: to maintain itself by self-sown seed
