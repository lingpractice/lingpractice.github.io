revolver

noun

re·​volv·​er ri-ˈväl-vər 
-ˈvȯl-,
 also  -ˈvä-vər,

Synonyms of revolver
1
: one that revolves
2
: a handgun with a cylinder of several chambers brought successively into line with the barrel and discharged with the same hammer
