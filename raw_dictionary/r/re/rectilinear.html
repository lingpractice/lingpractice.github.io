rectilinear

adjective

rec·​ti·​lin·​e·​ar ˌrek-tə-ˈli-nē-ər 
1
: moving in or forming a straight line
rectilinear motion
2
: characterized by straight lines
the rectilinear skyline of a modern city
3
: perpendicular sense 3
rectilinearity
ˌrek-tə-ˌli-nē-ˈer-ə-tē 
-ˈa-rə-tē
noun
rectilinearly adverb
