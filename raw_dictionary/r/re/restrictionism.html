restrictionism

noun

re·​stric·​tion·​ism ri-ˈstrik-shə-ˌni-zəm 
: a policy or philosophy favoring restriction (as of trade or immigration)
restrictionist
ri-ˈstrik-sh(ə-)nist 
adjective or noun
