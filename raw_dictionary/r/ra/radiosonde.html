radiosonde

noun

ra·​dio·​sonde ˈrā-dē-ō-ˌsänd 
plural radiosondes
: a miniature radio transmitter that is carried aloft (as by an unpiloted balloon) with instruments for sensing and broadcasting atmospheric conditions
