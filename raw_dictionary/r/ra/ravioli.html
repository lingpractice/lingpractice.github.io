ravioli

noun

rav·​i·​o·​li ˌra-vē-ˈō-lē 
ˌrä-
plural ravioli also raviolis ˌra-vē-ˈō-lēz 
ˌrä-
: pasta in the form of little cases of dough containing a savory filling (as of meat or cheese)
