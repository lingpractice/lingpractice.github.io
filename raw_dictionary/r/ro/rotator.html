rotator

noun

ro·​ta·​tor ˈrō-ˌtā-tər 
 also  rō-ˈtā-
: one that rotates or causes rotation
especially, plural rotators or rotatores ˌrō-tə-ˈtȯr-ˌēz  : a muscle that partially rotates a part on its axis
