bystander

noun

by·​stand·​er ˈbī-ˌstan-dər 
Synonyms of bystander
: one who is present but not taking part in a situation or event : a chance spectator
innocent bystanders who were injured in the shooting

Synonyms

  • observer
  • onlooker
  • spectator
  • viewer
  • watcher
