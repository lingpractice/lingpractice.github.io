biracial

adjective

bi·​ra·​cial (ˌ)bī-ˈrā-shəl 
: of, relating to, or involving members of two races (see race entry 1 sense 1a)
a biracial coalition
biracial couples
also : having parents from two races
biracial children
biracialism
(ˌ)bī-ˈrā-shə-ˌli-zəm 
noun
