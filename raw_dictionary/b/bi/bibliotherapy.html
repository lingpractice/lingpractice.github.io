bibliotherapy

noun

bib·​lio·​ther·​a·​py ˌbi-blē-ə-ˈther-ə-pē 
-ˈthe-rə-
: the use of reading materials for help in solving personal problems or for psychiatric therapy
also : the reading materials so used
