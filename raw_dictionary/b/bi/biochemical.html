biochemical

adjective

bio·​chem·​i·​cal ˌbī-ō-ˈke-mi-kəl 
1
: of or relating to biochemistry
2
: characterized by, produced by, or involving chemical reactions in living organisms
a biochemical defect in the brain
biochemical noun
biochemically
ˌbī-ō-ˈke-mi-k(ə-)lē 
adverb
