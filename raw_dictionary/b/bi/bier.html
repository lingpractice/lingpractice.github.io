bier

noun

ˈbir 
Synonyms of bier
1
: a stand on which a corpse or coffin is placed
also : a coffin together with its stand
2
archaic : a framework for carrying

Synonyms

  • box
  • casket
  • coffin
  • pall
  • sarcophagus
