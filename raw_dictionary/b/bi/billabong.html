billabong

noun

bil·​la·​bong ˈbi-lə-ˌbȯŋ 
-ˌbäŋ
Synonyms of billabong
1
Australia
a
: a blind channel leading out from a river
b
: a usually dry streambed that is filled seasonally
2
Australia : a backwater forming a stagnant pool

Synonyms

  • mere [chiefly British]
  • pool
  • puddle
  • stank [British dialect]
  • well
