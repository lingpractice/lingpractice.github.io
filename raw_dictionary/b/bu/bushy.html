bushy

adjective

ˈbu̇-shē 
bushier; bushiest
1
: full of or overgrown with bushes
2
: resembling a bush
especially : being thick and spreading
bushily
ˈbu̇-shə-lē 
adverb
bushiness
ˈbu̇-shē-nəs 
noun
