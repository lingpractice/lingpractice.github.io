buccal

adjective

buc·​cal ˈbə-kəl 
1
: of, relating to, near, involving, or supplying a cheek
the buccal surface of a tooth
the buccal branch of the facial nerve
2
: of, relating to, involving, or lying in the mouth
the buccal cavity
buccally
ˈbə-kə-lē 
adverb
