barberry

noun

bar·​ber·​ry ˈbär-ˌber-ē 
-ˌbe-rē
: any of a genus (Berberis of the family Berberidaceae, the barberry family) of shrubs usually having spines, usually yellow flowers, and oblong red or blackish berries
