bauxite

noun

baux·​ite ˈbȯk-ˌsīt 
ˈbäk-
: an impure mixture of earthy hydrous aluminum oxides and hydroxides that is the principal source of aluminum
bauxitic
bȯk-ˈsi-tik 
bäk-
adjective
