ballyhoo

noun

bal·​ly·​hoo ˈba-lē-ˌhü

Synonyms of ballyhoo
1
: a noisy attention-getting demonstration or talk
2
: flamboyant, exaggerated, or sensational promotion or publicity
3
: excited commotion
