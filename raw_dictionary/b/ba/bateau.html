bateau

noun

ba·​teau ba-ˈtō 
variants or less commonly batteau
plural bateaux ba-ˈtō(z)  also batteaux
Synonyms of bateau
: any of various small craft
especially : a flat-bottomed boat with raked bow and stern and flaring sides
