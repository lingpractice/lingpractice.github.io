basketful

noun

bas·​ket·​ful ˈba-skit-ˌfu̇l 
plural basketfuls ˈba-skit-ˌfu̇lz  also basketsful ˈba-skits-ˌfu̇l 
Synonyms of basketful

also : a considerable quantity
