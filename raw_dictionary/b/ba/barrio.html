barrio

noun

bar·​rio ˈbär-ē-ˌō 
ˈber-,
ˈba-rē- 
plural barrios
Synonyms of barrio
1
: a ward, quarter, or district of a city or town in a Spanish-speaking country
2
: a Spanish-speaking quarter or neighborhood in a city or town in the U.S. especially in the Southwest
