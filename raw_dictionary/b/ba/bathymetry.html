bathymetry

noun

ba·​thym·​e·​try bə-ˈthi-mə-trē 
plural bathymetries
: the measurement of water depth at various places in a body of water
also : the information derived from such measurements
bathymetric
ˌba-thi-ˈme-trik 
adjective
or less commonly bathymetrical
ˌba-thi-ˈme-tri-kəl 
bathymetrically
ˌba-thi-ˈme-tri-k(ə-)lē 
adverb
