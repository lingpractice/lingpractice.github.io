battalion

noun

bat·​tal·​ion bə-ˈtal-yən 
Synonyms of battalion
1
: a considerable body of troops organized to act together : army
2
: a military unit composed of a headquarters and two or more companies, batteries, or similar units
3
: a large group

Synonyms

  • army
  • array
  • host
  • legion
