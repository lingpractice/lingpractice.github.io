bounden

adjective

bound·​en ˈbau̇n-dən 
Synonyms of bounden
1
archaic : being under obligation : beholden
2
: made obligatory : binding
our bounden duty

Synonyms

  • beholden
  • indebted
  • obligated
  • obliged
