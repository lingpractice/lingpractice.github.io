bobtail

noun

bob·​tail ˈbäb-ˌtāl 
1
a
: a bobbed tail
b
: a horse, dog, or cat with a bobbed or very short tail
especially : old english sheepdog
2
: something curtailed
bobtail adjective
or bobtailed
ˈbäb-ˌtāld
