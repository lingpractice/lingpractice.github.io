bookman

noun

book·​man ˈbu̇k-mən 
Synonyms of bookman
1
: a person who has a love of books and especially of reading
2
: a person who is involved in the writing, publishing, or selling of books

Synonyms

  • bibliolater
  • bibliomaniac
  • bibliophile
