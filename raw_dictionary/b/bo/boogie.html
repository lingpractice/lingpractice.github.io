Synonyms of boogie

verb

boo·​gie ˈbu̇-gē  ˈbü- 
variants or less commonly boogy or boogey
boogied also boogeyed; boogying also boogeying

intransitive verb

1
: to dance to rock music
also : revel, party
2
a
: to move quickly
b
: to get going
