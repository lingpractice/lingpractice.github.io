bodhisattva

noun

bo·​dhi·​satt·​va ˌbō-di-ˈsət-və  -ˈsät- 
variants or boddhisattva
: a being that compassionately refrains from entering nirvana in order to save others and is worshipped as a deity in Mahayana Buddhism
