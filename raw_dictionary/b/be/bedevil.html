bedevil

verb

be·​dev·​il bi-ˈde-vəl 
bē-
bedeviled; bedeviling; bedevils
Synonyms of bedevil

transitive verb

1
: to possess with or as if with a devil
2
: to cause distress : trouble
3
: to change for the worse : spoil
4
: to confuse utterly
