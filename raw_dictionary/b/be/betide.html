betide

verb

be·​tide bi-ˈtīd 
bē-
betided; betiding; betides
Synonyms of betide

intransitive verb

: to happen especially as if by fate

transitive verb

: to happen to : befall
—used chiefly in the phrase woe betide
woe betide our enemies
