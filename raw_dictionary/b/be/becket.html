becket

noun

beck·​et ˈbe-kət 
Synonyms of becket
: a device for holding something in place: such as
a
: a grommet or a loop of rope with a knot at one end to catch in an eye at the other
b
: a ring of rope or metal
c
: a loop of rope (as for a handle)
