beady

adjective

ˈbē-dē 
beadier; beadiest
1
a
: resembling beads
b
: small, round, and shiny with interest or greed
beady eyes
2
: marked by bubbles or beads
a beady liquor
beadily
ˈbē-də-lē 
adverb
