befoul

verb

be·​foul bi-ˈfau̇(-ə)l 
bē-
befouled; befouling; befouls
Synonyms of befoul

transitive verb

1
: to make foul (as with dirt or waste)
2
: sully, soil, besmirch
scandal befouled his reputation
