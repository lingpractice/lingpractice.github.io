bronzed

adjective

ˈbränzd 
Synonyms of bronzed
1
: made tawny or brown by exposure to the sun : tanned
bronzed skin
… holiday homes and shopwindows hopefully displaying images of bronzed beauties.—John Updike
2
: coated with a substance to create the appearance of bronze
a bronzed sculpture
bronzed baby shoes
