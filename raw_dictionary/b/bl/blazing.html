blazing

adjective

blaz·​ing ˈblā-ziŋ 
Synonyms of blazing
1
: burning very brightly and intensely
a blazing fire
2
: of outstanding power, speed, heat, or intensity
blazing eyes
a blazing fastball
blazing gunfire
blazingly adverb
