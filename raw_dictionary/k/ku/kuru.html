kuru

noun

ku·​ru ˈku̇r-(ˌ)ü 
: a rare progressive fatal prion disease that resembles Creutzfeldt-Jakob disease and has occurred among tribespeople in eastern New Guinea who engaged in a form of ritual cannibalism
