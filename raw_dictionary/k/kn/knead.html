knead

verb

ˈnēd 
kneaded; kneading; kneads
Synonyms of knead

transitive verb

1
a
: to work and press into a mass with or as if with the hands
kneading dough
b
: to manipulate or massage with a kneading motion
kneaded sore neck muscles
2
: to form or shape by or as if by kneading
kneadable
ˈnē-də-bəl 
adjective
kneader noun
