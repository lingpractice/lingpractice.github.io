ketoconazole

noun

ke·​to·​co·​na·​zole ˌkē-tō-ˈkō-nə-ˌzōl 
: a synthetic broad-spectrum antifungal agent C26H28Cl2N4O4 used to treat chronic internal and cutaneous infections
