horseman

noun

horse·​man ˈhȯrs-mən 
Synonyms of horseman
1
: a rider or driver of horses
especially : one whose skill is exceptional
2
: a person skilled in caring for or managing horses
3
: a person who breeds or raises horses
horsemanship
ˈhȯrs-mən-ˌship 
noun
