houndstooth

noun

hounds·​tooth ˈhau̇n(d)z-ˌtüth 
variants or less commonly hound's-tooth
: a usually small broken-check textile pattern
also : a fabric woven in this pattern

called also houndstooth check, hound's-tooth check
