hootenanny

noun

hoo·​te·​nan·​ny ˈhü-tə-ˌna-nē 
plural hootenannies
Synonyms of hootenanny
1
chiefly dialectal : gadget
2
: a gathering at which folk singers entertain often with the audience joining in
