honeybee

noun

hon·​ey·​bee ˈhə-nē-ˌbē 
variants or honey bee
: a honey-producing bee (genus Apis of the family Apidae)
especially : a European bee (A. mellifera) introduced worldwide and kept in hives for the honey it produces
