hydroid

1 of 2

adjective

hy·​droid ˈhī-ˌdrȯid 
: of or relating to a hydrozoan
especially : resembling a typical hydra

hydroid

2 of 2

noun

: hydrozoan
especially : a hydrozoan polyp as distinguished from a hydrozoan medusa
