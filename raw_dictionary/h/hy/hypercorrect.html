hypercorrect

adjective

hy·​per·​cor·​rect ˌhī-pər-kə-ˈrekt 
: of, relating to, or characterized by the production of a nonstandard linguistic form or construction on the basis of a false analogy (such as "badly" in "my eyes have gone badly")
hypercorrection
ˌhī-pər-kə-ˈrek-shən 
noun
hypercorrectly
ˌhī-pər-kə-ˈrek-(t)lē 
adverb
hypercorrectness
ˌhī-pər-kə-ˈrek(t)-nəs 
noun
