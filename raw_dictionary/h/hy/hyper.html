hyper

1 of 2

adjective

hy·​per ˈhī-pər 
Synonyms of hyper
1
: high-strung, excitable
also : highly excited
was a little hyper after drinking too much coffee
2
: extremely active
hyper children

hyper-

2 of 2

prefix

1
: above : beyond : super-
hypermarket
2
a
: excessively
hypersensitive
b
: excessive
hyperemia
3
: that is or exists in a space of more than three dimensions
hyperspace
4
: bridging points within an entity (such as a database or network) nonsequentially
hypertext
