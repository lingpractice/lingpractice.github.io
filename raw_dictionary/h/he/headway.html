headway

noun

head·​way ˈhed-ˌwā 
Synonyms of headway
1
a
: motion or rate of motion in a forward direction
b
: advance, progress
2
: headroom (as under an arch) sufficient to allow passage
3
: the time interval between two vehicles traveling in the same direction on the same route
