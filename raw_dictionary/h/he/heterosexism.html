heterosexism

noun

het·​ero·​sex·​ism ˌhe-tə-rō-ˈsek-si-zəm 
: discrimination or prejudice against nonheterosexual people based on the belief that heterosexuality is the only normal and natural expression of sexuality
heterosexist
ˌhe-tə-rō-ˈsek-sist
