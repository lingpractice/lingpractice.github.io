heartily

adverb

heart·​i·​ly ˈhär-tə-lē 
Synonyms of heartily
1
: in a hearty manner
2
a
: with all sincerity : wholeheartedly
b
: with zest or gusto
3
: wholly, thoroughly
heartily sick of all this talk
