headland

noun

head·​land ˈhed-lənd 
-ˌland
Synonyms of headland
1
: unplowed land at the ends of furrows or near a fence
2
: a point of usually high land jutting out into a body of water : promontory

Synonyms

  • ness
  • point
  • promontory
