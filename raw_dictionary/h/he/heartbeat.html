heartbeat

noun

heart·​beat ˈhärt-ˌbēt 
Synonyms of heartbeat
1
: one complete pulsation of the heart
2
: the vital center or driving impulse
3
: a brief space of time : flash
—used chiefly in the phrase in a heartbeat
