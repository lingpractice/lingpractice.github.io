hemicellulose

noun

hemi·​cel·​lu·​lose ˌhe-mi-ˈsel-yə-ˌlōs 
-ˌlōz
: any of various plant polysaccharides less complex than cellulose and easily hydrolyzable to monosaccharides and other products
