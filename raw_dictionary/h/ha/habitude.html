habitude

noun

hab·​i·​tude ˈha-bə-ˌtüd 
-ˌtyüd
Synonyms of habitude
1
a
: habitual disposition or mode of behavior or procedure
b
: custom
2
archaic : native or essential character
3
obsolete : habitual association
