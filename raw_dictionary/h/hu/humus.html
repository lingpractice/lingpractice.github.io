humus

noun

hu·​mus ˈhyü-məs 
ˈyü-
Synonyms of humus
geology : a brown or black complex variable material resulting from partial decomposition of plant or animal matter and forming the organic (see organic entry 1 sense 1a(2)) portion of soil
