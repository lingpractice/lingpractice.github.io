lucidity

noun

lu·​cid·​i·​ty lü-ˈsi-də-tē 
Synonyms of lucidity
1
: clearness of thought or style
the lucidity of the explanation
2
: a presumed capacity to perceive the truth directly and instantaneously : clairvoyance
when the spirit is drawn to lucidity by the immediacy of death—Graham Greene

Synonyms

  • clarity
  • explicitness
  • lucidness
  • perspicuity
  • perspicuousness
  • simplicity
