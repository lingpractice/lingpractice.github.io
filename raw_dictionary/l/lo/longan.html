longan

noun

lon·​gan ˈlȯŋ-gən 
-ən
1
: a pulpy fruit related to the lychee and produced by a southeast Asian evergreen tree (Euphoria longan synonym Dimocarpus longan) of the soapberry family
2
: a tree that bears the longan
