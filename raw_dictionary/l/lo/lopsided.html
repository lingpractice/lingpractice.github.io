lopsided

adjective

lop·​sid·​ed ˈläp-ˌsī-dəd 
Synonyms of lopsided
1
: leaning to one side
2
: lacking in balance, symmetry, or proportion : disproportionately heavy on one side
a lopsided vote of 99–1
lopsidedly adverb
lopsidedness noun
