lowlife

noun

low·​life ˈlō-ˌlīf 
plural lowlifes ˈlō-ˌlīfs  also lowlives ˈlō-ˌlīvz 
Synonyms of lowlife
1
: a person of low social status
2
: a person of low moral character
low-life adjective
