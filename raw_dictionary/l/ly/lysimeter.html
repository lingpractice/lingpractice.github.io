lysimeter

noun

ly·​sim·​e·​ter lī-ˈsi-mə-tər 
: a device for measuring the percolation of water through soils and for determining the soluble constituents removed in the drainage
lysimetric
ˌlī-sə-ˈme-trik 
adjective
