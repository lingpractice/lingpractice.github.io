laxative

1 of 2

adjective

lax·​a·​tive ˈlak-sə-tiv 
: having a tendency to loosen or relax
specifically : producing bowel movements and relieving constipation

laxative

2 of 2

noun

: a usually mild laxative drug

Word History

Etymology

Adjective

Adjective
