labdanum

noun

lab·​da·​num ˈlab-də-nəm 
variants or less commonly ladanum
ˈla-də-nəm 
ˈlad-nəm
: a soft dark fragrant bitter oleoresin derived from various rockroses (genus Cistus) and used in making perfumes
