landlord

noun

land·​lord ˈland-ˌlȯrd 
Synonyms of landlord
1
: the owner of property (such as land, houses, or apartments) that is leased or rented to another
2
: the master of an inn or lodging house : innkeeper

Synonyms

  • lessor
  • letter
  • renter
