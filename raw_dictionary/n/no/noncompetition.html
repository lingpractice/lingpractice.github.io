noncompetition

noun

non·​com·​pe·​ti·​tion ˌnän-ˌkäm-pə-ˈti-shən 
: an absence or lack of competition
They want their Ambers and their Alexanders to grow up in a cozy womb of noncompetition …—Rick Reilly
—usually used before another noun
