noncombustible

adjective

non·​com·​bus·​ti·​ble ˌnän-kəm-ˈbə-stə-bəl 
Synonyms of noncombustible
: incapable of igniting and burning when subjected to fire : not combustible
noncombustible materials
drums of noncombustible waste

Synonyms

  • fireproof
  • incombustible
  • nonflammable
  • noninflammable
