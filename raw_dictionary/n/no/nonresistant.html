nonresistant

adjective

non·​re·​sis·​tant ˌnän-ri-ˈzi-stənt 
Synonyms of nonresistant
: not resistant
specifically : susceptible to the effects of a deleterious agent (such as an insecticide, a pathogen, or an extreme environmental condition)
nonresistant noun
