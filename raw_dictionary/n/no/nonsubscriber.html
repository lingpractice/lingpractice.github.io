nonsubscriber

noun

non·​sub·​scrib·​er ˌnän-səb-ˈskrī-bər 
plural nonsubscribers
: one who does not subscribe to something (such as a magazine)
Print subscribers get to see the online classifieds before nonsubscribers …—Jennie L. Phipps
