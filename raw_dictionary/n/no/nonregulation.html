nonregulation

1 of 2

noun

non·​reg·​u·​la·​tion ˌnän-ˌre-gyə-ˈlā-shən 
 also  -ˌrā-
: an absence or lack of regulation : the failure or refusal to regulate
the nonregulation of the financial markets
