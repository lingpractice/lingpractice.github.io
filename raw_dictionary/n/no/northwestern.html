northwestern

adjective

north·​west·​ern nȯr(th)-ˈwe-stərn 
1
often capitalized : of, relating to, or characteristic of a region conventionally designated Northwest
2
: lying toward or coming from the northwest
northwesternmost
nȯr(th)-ˈwe-stərn-ˌmōst
