nonintervention

noun

non·​in·​ter·​ven·​tion ˌnän-ˌin-tər-ˈven(t)-shən 
: the state or policy of not intervening
nonintervention in the affairs of other countries
noninterventionist
ˌnän-ˌin-tər-ˈven(t)-sh(ə-)nist 
noun or adjective
