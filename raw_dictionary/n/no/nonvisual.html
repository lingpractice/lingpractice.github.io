nonvisual

adjective

non·​vi·​su·​al ˌnän-ˈvi-zhə-wəl 
-zhəl;
-ˈvizh-wəl
: not visual: such as
a
: not of, relating to, or used in vision
the nonvisual senses
b
: not attained or maintained by sight
nonvisual information
