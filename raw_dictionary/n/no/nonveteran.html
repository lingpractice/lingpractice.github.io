nonveteran

noun

non·​vet·​er·​an ˌnän-ˈve-tə-rən 
-ˈve-trən
plural nonveterans
: a person who is not a former member of the armed forces : someone who is not a military veteran
But he has pledged to shrink the size of government, which would affect veterans and nonveterans alike.—Lisa Rein
