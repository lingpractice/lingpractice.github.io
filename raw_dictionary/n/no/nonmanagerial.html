nonmanagerial

adjective

non·​man·​a·​ge·​ri·​al ˌnän-ˌma-nə-ˈjir-ē-əl 
Synonyms of nonmanagerial
: not of or relating to a manager or group of managers : not managerial
a nonmanagerial role in the company
nonmanagerial workers
