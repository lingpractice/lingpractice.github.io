nutty

adjective

nut·​ty ˈnə-tē 
nuttier; nuttiest
Synonyms of nutty
1
: having or producing nuts
nutty trees
2
: having a flavor like that of nuts
a nutty sauce
3
: eccentric, silly
a nutty professor
nutty ideas
also, sometimes offensive : mentally unsound
