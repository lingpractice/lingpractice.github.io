napalm

1 of 2

noun

na·​palm ˈnā-ˌpäm  -ˌpälm 
 also  ˈna-,
 also  nə-ˈpä(l)m
1
: a thickener consisting of a mixture of aluminum soaps used in jelling gasoline (as for incendiary bombs)
2
: fuel jelled with napalm

napalm

2 of 2

verb

napalmed; napalming; napalms

transitive verb

: to assault with napalm
