nitrify

transitive verb

ni·​tri·​fy ˈnī-trə-ˌfī 
-ed/-ing/-es
1
: to combine or impregnate with nitrogen or a nitrogen compound
2
: to convert by oxidation into nitric acid or nitrous acid or their salts : subject to or produce by nitrification compare nitrosify

Word History

Etymology

French nitrifier, from nitre niter + -ifier -ify
