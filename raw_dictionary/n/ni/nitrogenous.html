nitrogenous

adjective

ni·​trog·​e·​nous nī-ˈträ-jə-nəs 
: relating to, being, or containing nitrogen
nitrogenous fertilizer
Phospholipids are compounds of neutral fat, a phosphoric acid, and a nitrogenous base.—Sue Rodwell Williams
