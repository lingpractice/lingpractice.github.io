neoliberal

noun

neo·​lib·​er·​al ˌnē-ō-ˈli-b(ə-)rəl 
: a liberal who de-emphasizes traditional liberal doctrines in order to seek progress by more pragmatic methods
neoliberal adjective
neoliberalism
ˌnē-ō-ˈli-b(ə-)rə-ˌli-zəm
