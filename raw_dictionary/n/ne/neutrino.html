neutrino

noun

neu·​tri·​no nü-ˈtrē-(ˌ)nō 
nyü-
plural neutrinos
: an uncharged elementary particle that is believed to have a very small mass, that has any of three forms, and that interacts only rarely with other particles
neutrinoless
nü-ˈtrē-(ˌ)nō-ləs 
nyü-
adjective
