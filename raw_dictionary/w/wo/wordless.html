wordless

adjective

word·​less ˈwərd-ləs 
Synonyms of wordless
1
: not expressed in or accompanied by words
a wordless picture book
2
: silent, speechless
sat wordless throughout the meeting
wordlessly adverb
wordlessness noun

Synonyms

  • silent
