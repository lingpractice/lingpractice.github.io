intransitive verb

1

: to close and open the eyelids quickly
3

4
: to gleam or flash intermittently : twinkle

5

b
: to stop shining
—usually used with out
6

transitive verb

1
: to cause to open and shut
2
: to affect or influence by or as if by blinking the eyes

Word History

Etymology

Verb
