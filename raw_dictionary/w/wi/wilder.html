wilder

verb

wil·​der ˈwil-dər 
wildered; wildering; wilders

transitive verb

1
archaic : to lead astray
2
archaic : bewilder, perplex

intransitive verb

archaic : to move at random : wander
wilderment
ˈwil-dər-mənt
