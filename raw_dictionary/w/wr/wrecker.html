wrecker

noun

wreck·​er ˈre-kər 
Synonyms of wrecker
1
a
: one that searches for or works on the wrecks of ships (as for rescue or for plunder)
b
: tow truck
2
: one that wrecks
especially : one whose work is the demolition of buildings
