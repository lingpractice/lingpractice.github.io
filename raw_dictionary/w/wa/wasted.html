wasted

adjective

wast·​ed ˈwā-stəd 
Synonyms of wasted
1
: laid waste : ravaged
2
: impaired in strength or health : emaciated
3
archaic : gone by : elapsed
the chronicle of wasted time—William Shakespeare
4
: unprofitably used, made, or expended
wasted effort
5
slang : intoxicated from drugs or alcohol
