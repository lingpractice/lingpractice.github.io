washy

adjective

ˈwȯ-shē 
ˈwä-
washier; washiest
Synonyms of washy
1
a
: weak, watery
washy tea
b
: deficient in color
c
: lacking in vigor, individuality, or definiteness
2
: lacking in condition and in firmness of flesh

Synonyms

  • dull
