warlike

adjective

war·​like ˈwȯr-ˌlīk 
Synonyms of warlike
1
obsolete : ready for war : equipped to fight
2
: fit for, disposed to, or fond of war : bellicose
a warlike people
3
: of, relating to, or useful in war
warlike preparations
4
: befitting or characteristic of war or a soldier
warlike cries
