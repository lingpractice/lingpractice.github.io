waterfall

noun

wa·​ter·​fall ˈwȯ-tər-ˌfȯl 
ˈwä-
Synonyms of waterfall
1
a
: a perpendicular or very steep descent of the water of a stream
b
: an artificial waterfall (as in a hotel lobby or a nightclub)
2
: something resembling a waterfall

Synonyms

  • cascade
  • cataract
  • fall(s)
