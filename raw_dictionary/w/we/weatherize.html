weatherize

verb

weath·​er·​ize ˈwe-t͟hə-ˌrīz 
weatherized; weatherizing

transitive verb

: to make (something, such as a house) better protected against winter weather (as by adding insulation)
weatherization
ˌwet͟h-rə-ˈzā-shən

noun
