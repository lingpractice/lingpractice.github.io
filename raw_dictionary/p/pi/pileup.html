pileup

noun

pile·​up ˈpī(-ə)l-ˌəp 
Synonyms of pileup
1
: a collision involving usually several motor vehicles
2
a
: a jammed tangled mass or pile (as of motor vehicles or people) resulting from collision or accumulation
b
: accumulation
