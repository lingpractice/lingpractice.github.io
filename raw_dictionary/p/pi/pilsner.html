pilsner

noun

pil·​sner ˈpil-snər 
ˈpilz-nər,
ˈpil-zə-nər 
variants or pilsener
plural pilsners or pilseners
1
: a lager with a strong flavor of hops
2
: a tall slender footed glass for beer
