pillbox

noun

pill·​box ˈpil-ˌbäks 
Synonyms of pillbox
1
: a box for pills
especially : a shallow round box of pasteboard
2
: a small low concrete emplacement for machine guns and antitank weapons
3
: a small round hat without a brim
specifically : a woman's shallow hat with a flat crown and straight sides
