plesiosaur

noun

ple·​si·​o·​saur ˈplē-sē-ə-ˌsȯr 
-zē-
: any of an order or suborder (Plesiosauria) of large carnivorous marine reptiles of the Mesozoic with dorsoventrally flattened bodies and limbs modified into flippers
