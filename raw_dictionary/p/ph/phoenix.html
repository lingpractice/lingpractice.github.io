phoenix

noun

phoe·​nix ˈfē-niks 
: a legendary bird which according to one account lived 500 years, burned itself to ashes on a pyre, and rose alive from the ashes to live another period
also : a person or thing likened to the phoenix
phoenixlike
ˈfē-niks-ˌlīk 
adjective
