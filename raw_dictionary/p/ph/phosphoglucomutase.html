phosphoglucomutase

noun

phos·​pho·​glu·​co·​mu·​tase ˌfäs-(ˌ)fō-ˌglü-kō-ˈmyü-ˌtās 
-ˌtāz
: an enzyme found in all plant and animal cells that catalyzes the reversible isomerization of glucose-1-phosphate to glucose-6-phosphate
