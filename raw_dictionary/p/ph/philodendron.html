philodendron

noun

phil·​o·​den·​dron ˌfi-lə-ˈden-drən 
plural philodendrons also philodendra ˌfi-lə-ˈden-drə 
: any of various aroid plants (as of the genus Philodendron) that are cultivated for their showy foliage
