pastitsio

noun

pas·​ti·​tsio pä-ˈstēt-sē-(ˌ)ō 
variants or less commonly pastitso
pä-ˈstēt-(ˌ)sō 
: a Greek baked dish made of ground meat layered with pasta and usually topped with white sauce and cheese
