paleoecology

noun

pa·​leo·​ecol·​o·​gy ˌpā-lē-ō-i-ˈkä-lə-jē 
-e-ˈkä-
: a branch of ecology that is concerned with the characteristics of ancient environments and with their relationships to ancient plants and animals
