pauper

noun

pau·​per ˈpȯ-pər 
Synonyms of pauper
1
: a person destitute of means except such as are derived from charity
specifically : one who receives aid from funds designated for the poor
paupers on welfare
2
: a very poor person
you'll end up a pauper on skid row—Robert Bixby
pauperism
ˈpȯ-pə-ˌri-zəm
