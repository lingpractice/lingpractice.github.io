patinate

verb

pat·​i·​nate ˈpa-tə-ˌnāt 
patinated; patinating

transitive verb

: to give a patina to

intransitive verb

: to take on a patina
—usually used in the past participle
patinated bronze
patination
ˌpa-tə-ˈnā-shən 
noun
