payroll

noun

pay·​roll ˈpā-ˌrōl 
Synonyms of payroll
1
: a paymaster's or employer's list of those entitled to pay and of the amounts due to each
2
: the sum necessary for distribution to those on a payroll
also : the money to be distributed
