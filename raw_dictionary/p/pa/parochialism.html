parochialism

noun

pa·​ro·​chi·​al·​ism pə-ˈrō-kē-ə-ˌli-zəm 
Synonyms of parochialism
: the quality or state of being parochial
especially : selfish pettiness or narrowness (as of interests, opinions, or views)
