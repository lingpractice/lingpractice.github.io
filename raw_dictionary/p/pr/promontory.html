promontory

noun

prom·​on·​to·​ry ˈprä-mən-ˌtȯr-ē 
plural promontories
Synonyms of promontory
1
a
: a high point of land or rock projecting into a body of water
b
: a prominent mass of land overlooking or projecting into a lowland
2
: a bodily prominence

Synonyms

  • headland
  • ness
  • point

Word History

First Known Use
