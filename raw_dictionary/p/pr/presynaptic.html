presynaptic

adjective

pre·​syn·​ap·​tic ˌprē-sə-ˈnap-tik 
: of, occurring in, or being a neuron by which a nerve impulse is conveyed to a synapse
a presynaptic membrane
a presynaptic neuron
presynaptically
ˌprē-sə-ˈnap-ti-k(ə-)lē 
adverb
