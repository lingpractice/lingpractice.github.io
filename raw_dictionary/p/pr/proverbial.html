proverbial

adjective

pro·​ver·​bi·​al prə-ˈvər-bē-əl 
1
: of, relating to, or resembling a proverb
2
: that has become a proverb or byword : commonly spoken of
the proverbial smoking gun
proverbially
prə-ˈvər-bē-ə-lē 
adverb
