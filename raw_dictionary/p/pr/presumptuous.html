presumptuous

adjective

pre·​sump·​tu·​ous pri-ˈzəm(p)-chə-wəs 
-chəs,
-shəs
Synonyms of presumptuous
: overstepping due bounds (as of propriety or courtesy) : taking liberties
presumptuously adverb
presumptuousness noun
