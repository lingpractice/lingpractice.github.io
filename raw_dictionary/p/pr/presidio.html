presidio

noun

pre·​si·​dio pri-ˈsē-dē-ˌō 
-ˈsi-,
-ˈzē-,
-ˈzi- 
plural presidios
: a garrisoned place
especially : a military post or fortified settlement in areas currently or originally under Spanish control
