prekindergarten

noun

pre·​kin·​der·​gar·​ten (ˈ)prē-ˈkin-dər-ˌgär-tᵊn

Synonyms of prekindergarten
1
: nursery school
2
: a class or program preceding kindergarten for children usually from three to four years old
