protoplanet

noun

pro·​to·​plan·​et ˈprō-tō-ˌpla-nət 
: a hypothetical whirling gaseous mass within a giant cloud of gas and dust that rotates around a sun and is believed to give rise to a planet
protoplanetary
ˌprō-tō-ˈpla-nə-ˌter-ē 
adjective
