praline

noun

pra·​line ˈprä-ˌlēn  ˈprā-  ˈprȯ- 
: a confection of nuts and sugar: such as
a
: almonds cooked in boiling sugar until brown and crisp
b
: a patty of creamy brown sugar and pecan meats
