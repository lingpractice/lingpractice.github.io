pedant

noun

ped·​ant ˈpe-dᵊnt 
Synonyms of pedant
1
a
: one who is unimaginative or who unduly emphasizes minutiae in the presentation or use of knowledge
b
: one who makes a show of knowledge
c
: a formalist or precisionist in teaching
2
obsolete : a male schoolteacher
