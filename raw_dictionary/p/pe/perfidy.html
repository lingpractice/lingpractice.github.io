perfidy

noun

per·​fi·​dy ˈpər-fə-dē 
plural perfidies
Synonyms of perfidy
1
: the quality or state of being faithless or disloyal : treachery
2
: an act or an instance of disloyalty

Synonyms

  • disloyalty
  • faithlessness
