pentagonal

adjective

pen·​tag·​o·​nal pen-ˈta-gə-nᵊl 
1
: having five sides and five angles
2
: having a pentagon as a cross section or as a base
a pentagonal pyramid
pentagonally
pen-ˈta-gə-nᵊl-ē 
adverb
