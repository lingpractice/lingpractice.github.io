petechia

noun

pe·​te·​chia pə-ˈtē-kē-ə 
plural petechiae pə-ˈtē-kē-ˌī 
: a minute reddish or purplish spot containing blood that appears in skin or mucous membrane as a result of localized hemorrhage
petechial
pə-ˈtē-kē-əl 
adjective
