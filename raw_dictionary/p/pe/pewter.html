pewter

noun

pew·​ter ˈpyü-tər 
Synonyms of pewter
1
: any of various alloys having tin as chief component
especially : a dull alloy with lead formerly used for domestic utensils
2
: utensils of pewter
3
: a bluish gray
pewter adjective
