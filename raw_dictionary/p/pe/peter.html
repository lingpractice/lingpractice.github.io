peter

1 of 3

verb

pe·​ter ˈpē-tər 
petered; petering; peters

intransitive verb

1
: to diminish gradually and come to an end : give out
—usually used with out
novelists whose creative impetus seems largely to have petered out—The Times Literary Supplement (London)
2
: to become exhausted
—usually used with out
