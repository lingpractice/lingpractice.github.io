pentimento

noun

pen·​ti·​men·​to ˌpen-tə-ˈmen-(ˌ)tō 
plural pentimenti
ˌpen-tə-ˈmen-(ˌ)tē
: a reappearance in a painting of an original drawn or painted element which was eventually painted over by the artist
