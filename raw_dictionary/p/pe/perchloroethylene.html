perchloroethylene

noun

per·​chlo·​ro·​eth·​y·​lene (ˌ)pər-ˌklȯr-ō-ˈe-thə-ˌlēn 
: a colorless nonflammable toxic liquid C2Cl4 used often as a solvent in dry cleaning and for removal of grease from metals
