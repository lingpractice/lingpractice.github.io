powerless

adjective

pow·​er·​less ˈpau̇(-ə)r-ləs 
Synonyms of powerless
1
: devoid of strength or resources
powerless victims
2
: lacking the authority or capacity to act
was powerless to help
powerlessly adverb
powerlessness noun
