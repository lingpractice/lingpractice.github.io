pollard

1 of 2

noun

pol·​lard ˈpä-lərd 
: a tree cut back to the trunk to promote the growth of a dense head of foliage

pollard

2 of 2

verb

pollarded; pollarding; pollards

transitive verb

: to make a pollard of (a tree)
