polemical

adjective

po·​lem·​i·​cal pə-ˈle-mi-kəl 
variants or less commonly polemic
pə-ˈle-mik 
Synonyms of polemical
1
: of, relating to, or being a polemic : controversial
2
: engaged in or addicted to polemics : disputatious
polemically
pə-ˈle-mi-k(ə-)lē 
adverb

Synonyms

  • quarrelsome
