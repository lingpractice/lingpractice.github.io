potted

adjective

pot·​ted ˈpä-təd 
Synonyms of potted
1
: preserved in a pot, jar, or can
potted meat
2
: planted or grown in a pot
3
: briefly and superficially summarized
a dull, pedestrian potted history—The Times Literary Supplement (London)
4
slang : drunk sense 1a
