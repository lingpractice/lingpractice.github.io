postdate

verb

post·​date ˌpōs(t)-ˈdāt 
ˈpōs(t)-ˌdāt
postdated; postdating; postdates
Synonyms of postdate

transitive verb

1
a
: to date with a date later than that of execution
postdate a check
b
: to assign (an event) to a date subsequent to that of actual occurrence
2
: to follow in time

Synonyms

  • follow
  • succeed
  • supervene
