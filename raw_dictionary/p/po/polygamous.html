polygamous

adjective

po·​lyg·​a·​mous pə-ˈli-gə-məs 
1
a
: relating to or practicing polygamy
b
: having more than one mate at one time
baboons are polygamous
2
: bearing both hermaphrodite and unisexual flowers on the same plant
