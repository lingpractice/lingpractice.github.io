posit

verb

pos·​it ˈpä-zət 
posited ˈpä-zə-təd 
ˈpäz-təd
 ; positing ˈpä-zə-tiŋ 
ˈpäz-tiŋ

transitive verb

1
: to dispose or set firmly : fix
2
: to assume or affirm the existence of : postulate
3
: to propose as an explanation : suggest
