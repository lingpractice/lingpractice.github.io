polka

noun

pol·​ka ˈpōl-kə  ˈpō-kə 
Synonyms of polka
1
: a lively couple dance of Bohemian origin in duple time with a basic pattern of hop-step-close-step
2
: a lively originally Bohemian dance tune in ²/₄ time
polka intransitive verb
