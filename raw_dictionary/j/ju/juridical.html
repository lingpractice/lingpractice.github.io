juridical

adjective

ju·​rid·​i·​cal ju̇-ˈri-di-kəl 
variants or less commonly juridic
ju̇-ˈri-dik 
1
: of or relating to the administration of justice or the office of a judge
2
: of or relating to law or jurisprudence : legal
juridically
ju̇-ˈri-di-k(ə-)lē 
adverb
