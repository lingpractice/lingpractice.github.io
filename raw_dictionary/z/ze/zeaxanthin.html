zeaxanthin

noun

ze·​a·​xan·​thin ˌzē-ə-ˈzan-thən 
: a yellow crystalline carotenoid alcohol C40H56O2 that is isomeric with lutein and occurs especially in fruits and vegetables (such as kiwifruit and spinach) and egg yolks
