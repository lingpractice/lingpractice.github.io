snaky

adjective

ˈsnā-kē 
variants or less commonly snakey
1
: of, formed of, or entwined with snakes
the Gorgon with snaky hair—Joseph Addison
2
: serpentine, snakelike
snaky coils
3
: suggestive of a snake
the oiliness and snaky insinuation of his demeanor—Thomas DeQuincy
4
: abounding in snakes
snakily
ˈsnā-kə-lē 
adverb
