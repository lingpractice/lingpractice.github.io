scarabaeoid

1 of 2

noun

scar·​a·​bae·​oid
plural -s
1
: a beetle of Scarabaeidae or a closely related family : scarabaeus
2
: scaraboid

scarabaeoid

2 of 2

adjective

1
: of, relating to, or constituting the third instar in the development of a blister beetle in which it resembles the larva of a scarabaeid beetle
2
: of or relating to the Scarabaeoidea

Word History

Etymology
