scraggly

adjective

scrag·​gly ˈskra-g(ə-)lē 
Synonyms of scraggly
: irregular in form or growth
scraggly hills
a scraggly beard
also : unkempt

Synonyms

  • broken
  • craggy
  • jagged
  • ragged
