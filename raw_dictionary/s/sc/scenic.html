scenic

adjective

sce·​nic ˈsē-nik 
 also  ˈse-
variants or less commonly scenical
ˈsē-ni-kəl 
 also  ˈse-
1
: of or relating to the stage, a stage setting, or stage representation
2
: of or relating to natural scenery
a scenic view
3
: representing an action, event, or episode
a scenic bas-relief
scenically
ˈsē-ni-k(ə-)lē 
 also  ˈse-
adverb
