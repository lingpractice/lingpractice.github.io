scrubby

adjective

scrub·​by ˈskrə-bē 
scrubbier; scrubbiest
Synonyms of scrubby
1
: inferior in size or quality : stunted
scrubby cattle
2
: covered with or consisting of scrub
3
: shabby, paltry
