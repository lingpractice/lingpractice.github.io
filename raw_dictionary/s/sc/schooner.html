schooner

noun

schoo·​ner ˈskü-nər 
Synonyms of schooner
1
: a typically 2-masted fore-and-aft rigged vessel with a foremast and a mainmast stepped nearly amidships
2
: a larger-than-usual drinking glass (as for beer)
