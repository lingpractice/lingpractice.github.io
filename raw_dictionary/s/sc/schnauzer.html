schnauzer

noun

schnau·​zer ˈshnau̇-zər 
ˈshnau̇t-sər
: a dog of any of three breeds that originated in Germany and are characterized by a wiry coat, long head, pointed ears, heavy eyebrows, and long hair on the muzzle:
a
: standard schnauzer
b
: giant schnauzer
c
: miniature schnauzer
