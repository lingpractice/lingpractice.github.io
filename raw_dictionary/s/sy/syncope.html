syncope

noun

syn·​co·​pe ˈsiŋ-kə-(ˌ)pē 
ˈsin-
Synonyms of syncope
1
: loss of consciousness resulting from insufficient blood flow to the brain : faint
2
: the loss of one or more sounds or letters in the interior of a word (as in fo'c'sle for forecastle)

Word History

Etymology

First Known Use
