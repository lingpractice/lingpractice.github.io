salesperson

noun

sales·​per·​son ˈsālz-ˌpər-sᵊn 
plural salespeople ˈsālz-ˌpē-pəl  also salespersons
Synonyms of salesperson
: a person whose job is to sell a product or service in a given territory, in a store, or by telephone : a salesman or saleswoman

Synonyms

  • clerk
  • salesclerk
  • salesman
