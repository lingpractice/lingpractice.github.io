salchow

noun

sal·​chow ˈsal-ˌkau̇ 
-ˌkȯv,
-(ˌ)kō
often capitalized
: a figure-skating jump with a takeoff from the back inside edge of one skate followed by one or more full turns in the air and a landing on the back outside edge of the opposite skate
