sagittal

adjective

sag·​it·​tal ˈsa-jə-tᵊl 
1
: of or relating to the suture between the parietal bones of the skull
2
: of, relating to, situated in, or being the median plane of the body or any plane parallel to it
sagittally
ˈsa-jə-tᵊl-ē 
adverb
