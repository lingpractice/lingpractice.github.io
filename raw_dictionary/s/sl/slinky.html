slinky

adjective

ˈsliŋ-kē 
slinkier; slinkiest
1
: characterized by slinking : stealthily quiet
slinky movements
2
: sleek and sinuous in movement or outline
especially : following the lines of the figure in a gracefully flowing manner
a slinky evening gown
slinkily
ˈsliŋ-kə-lē 
adverb
slinkiness
ˈsliŋ-kē-nəs 
noun
