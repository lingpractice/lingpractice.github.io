slither

verb

slith·​er ˈsli-t͟hər 
slithered; slithering; slithers
Synonyms of slither

intransitive verb

1
: to slide on or as if on a loose gravelly surface
2
: to slip or slide like a snake

transitive verb

: to cause to slide

Synonyms
