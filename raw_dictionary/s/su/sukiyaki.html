sukiyaki

noun

su·​ki·​ya·​ki skē-ˈyä-kē 
ˌsu̇-kē-ˈyä-,
ˌsü- 
: a dish consisting of thin slices of meat, tofu, and vegetables cooked in soy sauce and sugar
