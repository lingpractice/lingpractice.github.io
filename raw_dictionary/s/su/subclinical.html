subclinical

adjective

sub·​clin·​i·​cal ˌsəb-ˈkli-ni-kəl 
: not detectable or producing effects that are not detectable by the usual clinical tests
a subclinical infection
subclinical cancer
subclinically
ˌsəb-ˈkli-ni-k(ə-)lē 
adverb
