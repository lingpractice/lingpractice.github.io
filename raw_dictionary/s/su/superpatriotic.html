superpatriotic

adjective

su·​per·​pa·​tri·​ot·​ic ˌsü-pər-ˌpā-trē-ˈä-tik 
 chiefly British  -ˌpa-
Synonyms of superpatriotic
: having or showing extreme patriotism
a group of superpatriotic Americans
superpatriotism
ˌsü-pər-ˈpā-trē-ə-ˌti-zəm
