supertanker

noun

su·​per·​tank·​er ˈsü-pər-ˌtaŋ-kər 
plural supertankers
Synonyms of supertanker
: an exceptionally large and fast cargo ship fitted with tanks for carrying liquid (such as oil) in bulk
