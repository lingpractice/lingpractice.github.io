surrealistic

adjective

sur·​re·​al·​is·​tic sə-ˌrē-ə-ˈli-stik 
 also  -ˌrā-
1
: of or relating to surrealism
2
: having a strange dreamlike atmosphere or quality like that of a surrealist painting
surrealistically
sə-ˌrē-ə-ˈli-sti-k(ə-)lē 
 also  -ˌrā-
adverb
