superheavyweight

noun

su·​per·​hea·​vy·​weight ˌsü-pər-ˈhe-vē-ˌwāt 
Synonyms of superheavyweight
: an athlete (such as an Olympic weight lifter, boxer, or wrestler) who competes in the heaviest class or division
