subsonic

adjective

sub·​son·​ic ˌsəb-ˈsä-nik 
1
: of, relating to, or being a speed less than that of sound in air
2
: moving, capable of moving, or utilizing air currents moving at a subsonic speed
3
: infrasonic sense 1
subsonically
ˌsəb-ˈsä-ni-k(ə-)lē 
adverb
