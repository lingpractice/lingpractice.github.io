superoctave

noun

su·​per·​octave
¦süpə(r)+
plural superoctaves
1
: the octave above a specific note
2
Super Octave or less commonly Superoctave plural Super Octaves also Superoctaves : a metal organ flue stop of 2′ pitch in the manuals and 4′ pitch in the pedals

called also Fifteenth

Word History

Etymology

super- + octave
