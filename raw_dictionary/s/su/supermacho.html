supermacho

adjective

su·​per·​ma·​cho ˌsü-pər-ˈmä-(ˌ)chō 
: extremely masculine or macho
Most of these films centered on a seamy urban milieu and a tough lead character—almost always a supermacho male—in such guises as street hustler, private detective, boxer or Vietnam War veteran.—David Mills
