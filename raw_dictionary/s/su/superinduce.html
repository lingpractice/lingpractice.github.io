superinduce

verb

su·​per·​in·​duce ˌsü-pər-in-ˈdüs 
-ˈdyüs
superinduced; superinducing; superinduces

transitive verb

1
: to introduce as an addition over or above something already existing
2
: bring on, induce
superinduction
ˌsü-pər-in-ˈdək-shən 
noun

Word History

Etymology

Latin superinducere, from super- + inducere to lead in — more at induce

First Known Use

circa 1575, in the meaning defined at sense 1
