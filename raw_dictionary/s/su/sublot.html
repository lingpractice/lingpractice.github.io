sublot

noun

sub·​lot ˈsəb-ˌlät 
variants or sub-lot
plural sublots or sub-lots
: a portion of a lot : a subdivision of a group of things that make up a unit
… investigators first had to catalog the booty. They spent four 14-hour days … separating everything into plastic bags, organizing it into lots and sublots, videotaping everything.—Ted Anthony
