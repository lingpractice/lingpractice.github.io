sundeck

noun

sun·​deck ˈsən-ˌdek 
Synonyms of sundeck
1
: the usually upper deck of a ship that is exposed to the most sun
2
: a roof, deck, or terrace for sunning

Synonyms

  • balcony
  • deck
  • terrace
