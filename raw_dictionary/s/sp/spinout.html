spinout

1 of 2

noun

spin·​out ˈspin-ˌau̇t 
: a rotational skid by an automobile that usually causes it to leave the roadway

spin out

2 of 2

verb

spun out; spinning out; spins out

intransitive verb

: to make a rotational skid in an automobile
