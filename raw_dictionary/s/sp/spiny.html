spiny

adjective

ˈspī-nē 
spinier; spiniest
Synonyms of spiny
1
: abounding with difficulties, obstacles, or annoyances : thorny
spiny problems
2
: covered or armed with spines
broadly : bearing spines, prickles, or thorns
3
: slender and pointed like a spine
spininess noun
