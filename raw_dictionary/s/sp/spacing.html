spacing

noun

spac·​ing ˈspā-siŋ 
Synonyms of spacing
1
a
: the act of providing with spaces or placing at intervals
b
: an arrangement in space
2
a
: a limited extent : space
b
: the distance between any two objects in a usually regularly arranged series
