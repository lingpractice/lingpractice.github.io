skittish

adjective

skit·​tish ˈski-tish 
Synonyms of skittish
1
a
: lively or frisky in action : capricious
b
: variable, fluctuating
2
: easily frightened : restive
a skittish horse
3
a
: coy, bashful
b
: marked by extreme caution : wary
skittish investors
skittishly adverb
skittishness noun
