sheepish

adjective

sheep·​ish ˈshē-pish 
Synonyms of sheepish
1
: resembling a sheep: such as
a
: meek, timid
b
: stupid
2
: affected by or showing embarrassment caused by consciousness of a fault
a sheepish grin
sheepishly adverb
sheepishness noun
