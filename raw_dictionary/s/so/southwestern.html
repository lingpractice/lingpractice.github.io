southwestern

adjective

south·​west·​ern sau̇(th)-ˈwe-stərn 
1
: lying toward or coming from the southwest
2
often capitalized : of, relating to, or characteristic of a region conventionally designated Southwest
southwesternmost
sau̇(th)-ˈwe-stərn-ˌmōst 
adjective
