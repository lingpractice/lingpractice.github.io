soapbox

noun

soap·​box ˈsōp-ˌbäks 
Synonyms of soapbox
: an improvised platform used by a self-appointed, spontaneous, or informal orator
broadly : something that provides an outlet for delivering opinions
soapbox adjective

Synonyms

  • forum
  • medium
  • outlet
  • platform
  • venue
