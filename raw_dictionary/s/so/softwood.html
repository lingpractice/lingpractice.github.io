softwood

1 of 2

noun

soft·​wood ˈsȯft-ˌwu̇d 
1
: the wood of a coniferous tree (such as a fir or pine) whether hard or soft as distinguished from that of an angiospermous tree
2
: a tree that yields softwood

softwood

2 of 2

adjective

: having or made of softwood
