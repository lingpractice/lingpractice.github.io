solidarity

noun

sol·​i·​dar·​i·​ty ˌsä-lə-ˈder-ə-tē 
-ˈda-rə-
Synonyms of solidarity
: unity (as of a group or class) that produces or is based on community of interests, objectives, and standards

Word History
