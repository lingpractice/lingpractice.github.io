sisal

noun

si·​sal ˈsī-səl 
-zəl
1
a
: a strong white fiber used especially for cordage and twine

called also sisal hemp

b
: a widely cultivated Mexican agave (Agave sisalana) whose leaves yield sisal
2
: any of several fibers similar to true sisal
