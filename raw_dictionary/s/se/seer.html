seer

noun

ˈsir 
ˈsē-ər
Synonyms of seer
1
: one that sees
2
a
: one that predicts events or developments
b
: a person credited with extraordinary moral and spiritual insight
3
: one that practices divination especially by concentrating on a glass or crystal globe
