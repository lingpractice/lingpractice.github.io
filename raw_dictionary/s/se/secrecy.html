secrecy

noun

se·​cre·​cy ˈsē-krə-sē 
plural secrecies
Synonyms of secrecy
1
: the condition of being hidden or concealed
2
: the habit or practice of keeping secrets or maintaining privacy or concealment

Synonyms

  • closeness
  • secretiveness
