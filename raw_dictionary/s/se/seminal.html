seminal

adjective

sem·​i·​nal ˈse-mə-nᵊl 
Synonyms of seminal
1
: of, relating to, or consisting of seed or semen
seminal discharge
2
: containing or contributing the seeds of later development : creative, original
a seminal book
seminally
ˈse-mə-nᵊl-ē 
adverb
