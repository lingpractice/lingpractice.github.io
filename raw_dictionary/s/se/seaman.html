seaman

noun

sea·​man ˈsē-mən 
Synonyms of seaman
1
: sailor, mariner
2
a
: any of the three ranks below petty officer in the navy or coast guard
b
: an enlisted man in the navy or coast guard ranking above a seaman apprentice and below a petty officer
