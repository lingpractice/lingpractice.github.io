semester

noun

se·​mes·​ter sə-ˈme-stər 
1
: either of the two usually 18-week periods of instruction into which an academic year is often divided
2
: a period of six months
semestral
sə-ˈme-strəl 
adjective
or semestrial
sə-ˈme-strē-əl
