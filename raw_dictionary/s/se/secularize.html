secularize

verb

sec·​u·​lar·​ize ˈse-kyə-lə-ˌrīz 
secularized; secularizing
Synonyms of secularize

transitive verb

1
: to make secular
2
: to transfer from ecclesiastical to civil or lay use, possession, or control
3
: to convert to or imbue with secularism
secularizer noun
