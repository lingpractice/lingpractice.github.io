seedling

noun

seed·​ling ˈsēd-liŋ 
1
: a young plant grown from seed
2
a
: a young tree before it becomes a sapling
b
: a nursery plant not yet transplanted
seedling adjective

Illustration of seedling

  • 1 primary root

  • 4 hypocotyl
