stamen

noun

sta·​men ˈstā-mən 
plural stamens also stamina ˈstā-mə-nə 
ˈsta-
: a microsporophyll of a seed plant
specifically : the pollen-producing male organ of a flower that consists of an anther and a filament see flower illustration
