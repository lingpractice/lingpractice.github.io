stapler

1 of 2

noun (1)

sta·​pler ˈstā-p(ə-)lər 
: one that deals in staple goods or in staple fiber

stapler

2 of 2

noun (2)

: one that inserts staples
especially : a small usually hand-operated device for inserting wire staples
