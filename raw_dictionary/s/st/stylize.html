stylize

verb

styl·​ize ˈstī(-ə)-ˌlīz 
stylized; stylizing

transitive verb

: to conform to a conventional style
specifically : to represent or design according to a style or stylistic pattern rather than according to nature or tradition
stylization
ˌstī-lə-ˈzā-shən 
noun
