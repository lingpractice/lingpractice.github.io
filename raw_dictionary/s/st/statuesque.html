statuesque

adjective

stat·​u·​esque ˌsta-chə-ˈwesk 
Synonyms of statuesque
: resembling a statue especially in dignity, shapeliness, or stillness
especially : having a tall and shapely form
a statuesque actress
statuesquely adverb
