stranglehold

noun

stran·​gle·​hold ˈstraŋ-gəl-ˌhōld 
Synonyms of stranglehold
1
: an illegal wrestling hold by which one's opponent is choked
2
: a force or influence that chokes or suppresses freedom of movement or expression
