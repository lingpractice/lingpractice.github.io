streusel

noun

streu·​sel ˈstrü-səl 
-zəl,
ˈstrȯi-,
ˈshtrȯi-
: a crumbly mixture of fat, sugar, and flour and sometimes nuts and spices that is used as topping or filling for cake

Word History

Etymology

First Known Use
