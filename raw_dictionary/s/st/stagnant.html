stagnant

adjective

stag·​nant ˈstag-nənt 
Synonyms of stagnant
1
a(1)
: not flowing in a current or stream

(2)
: without inflow and outflow
a stagnant pool
b
: stale
long disuse had made the air stagnant and foul—Bram Stoker
2
: not advancing or developing
