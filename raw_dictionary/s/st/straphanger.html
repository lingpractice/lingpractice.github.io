straphanger

noun

strap·​hang·​er ˈstrap-ˌhaŋ-ər 
: a standing passenger in a subway, streetcar, bus, or train who clings for support to one of the short straps or similar devices placed along the aisle
straphang
ˈstrap-ˌhaŋ 
intransitive verb
