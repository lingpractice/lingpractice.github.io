storehouse

noun

store·​house ˈstȯr-ˌhau̇s 
Synonyms of storehouse
1
: a building for storing goods (such as provisions) : magazine, warehouse
2
: an abundant supply or source : repository
a storehouse of information

Synonyms

  • depository
  • depot
  • magazine
  • repository
  • storage
  • warehouse
