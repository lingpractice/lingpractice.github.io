tutti

1 of 2

adjective or adverb

tut·​ti ˈtü-tē 
ˈtu̇-;
ˈtü-ˌtē,
ˈtu̇- 
: with all voices or instruments performing together
—used as a direction in music

tutti

2 of 2

noun

: a passage or section performed by all the performers
