Synonyms of tumult

3
a
: violent agitation of mind or feelings
b
: a violent outburst

Word History

Etymology

Middle English tumulte, from Anglo-French, from Latin tumultus; perhaps akin to Sanskrit tumula noisy

First Known Use

15th century, in the meaning defined at sense 1a
