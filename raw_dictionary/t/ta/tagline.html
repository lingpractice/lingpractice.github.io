tagline

noun

tag·​line ˈtag-ˌlīn 
Synonyms of tagline
1
: a reiterated phrase identified with an individual, group, or product : slogan
2
: a final line (as in a play or joke)
especially : one that serves to clarify a point or create a dramatic effect
