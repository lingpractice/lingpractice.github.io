topsail

noun

top·​sail ˈtäp-ˌsāl 
-səl
variants or less commonly tops'l
ˈtäp-səl 
1
: the sail next above the lowermost sail on a mast in a square-rigged ship
2
: the sail set above and sometimes on the gaff in a fore-and-aft rigged ship
