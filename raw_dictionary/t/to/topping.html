topping

1 of 2

noun

top·​ping ˈtä-piŋ 
Synonyms of topping
1
: something that forms a top
especially : a garnish (such as a sauce, bread crumbs, or whipped cream) placed on top of a food for flavor or decoration
2
: the action of one that tops
3
: something removed by topping
