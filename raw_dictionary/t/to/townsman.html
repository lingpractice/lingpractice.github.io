townsman

noun

towns·​man ˈtau̇nz-mən 
Synonyms of townsman
1
a
: a native or resident of a town or city
b
: an urban or urbane person
2
: a fellow citizen of a town

Synonyms

  • burgher
  • citizen
  • townie
  • towny
  • villager
