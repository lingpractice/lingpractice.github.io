trustbuster

noun

trust·​bust·​er ˈtrəs(t)-ˌbə-stər 
: one who seeks to break up business trusts
specifically : a federal official who prosecutes trusts under the antitrust laws
trust-busting
ˈtrəs(t)-ˌbə-stiŋ 
noun
