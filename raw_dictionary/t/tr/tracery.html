tracery

noun

trac·​ery ˈtrā-sə-rē 
ˈtrās-rē
plural traceries
1
: architectural ornamental work with branching lines
especially : decorative openwork in a Gothic window
2
: a decorative interlacing of lines suggestive of Gothic tracery
traceried
ˈtrā-sə-rēd 
ˈtrās-rēd
