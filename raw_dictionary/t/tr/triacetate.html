triacetate

noun

tri·​ac·​e·​tate (ˌ)trī-ˈa-sə-ˌtāt 
1
: an acetate containing three CH3COO− groups
2
: a textile fiber or fabric consisting of cellulose that is completely or almost completely acetylated
