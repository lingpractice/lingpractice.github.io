triumphant

adjective

tri·​um·​phant trī-ˈəm(p)-fənt 
Synonyms of triumphant
1
: victorious, conquering
triumphant armies
2
: rejoicing for or celebrating victory
a triumphant shout
3
: notably successful
a triumphant performance
4
archaic : of or relating to a triumph
triumphantly adverb

Synonyms

  • flourishing
  • going
  • palmy
  • prosperous
  • successful
  • thriving
