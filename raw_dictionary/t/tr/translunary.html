translunary

adjective

trans·​lu·​nary
ˈtran(t)slüˌnerē,
-nzl-;
(ˈ)tran(t)¦slünərē,
-nz¦l-
: located beyond the moon : ethereal, visionary compare sublunary
who can imagine a translunary visitor in Times Square—O. S. J. Gogarty
his high translunary dreams—John Buchan

Word History

Etymology

trans- + Latin luna moon + English -ary
