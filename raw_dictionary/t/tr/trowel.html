trowel

1 of 2

noun

trow·​el ˈtrau̇(-ə)l 
: any of various hand tools used to apply, spread, shape, or smooth loose or plastic material
also : a scoop-shaped or flat-bladed garden tool for taking up and setting small plants

trowel

verb
