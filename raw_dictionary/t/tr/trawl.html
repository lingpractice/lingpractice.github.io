verb

ˈtrȯl 
trawled; trawling; trawls

intransitive verb

1
a
: to fish with a trawl
b
: to make a search as if by trawling
2
: troll sense 1a

transitive verb

: to catch (fish) with a trawl

trawl

2 of 2

noun

1
: a large conical net dragged along the sea bottom in gathering fish or other marine life
2
: setline
