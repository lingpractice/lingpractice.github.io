tidal

adjective

tid·​al ˈtī-dᵊl 
1
a
: of, relating to, caused by, or having tides
tidal cycles
tidal erosion
b
: periodically rising and falling or flowing and ebbing
tidal waters
2
: dependent (as to the time of arrival or departure) upon the state of the tide
a tidal steamer
tidally
ˈtī-dᵊl-ē 
adverb
