ticklish

adjective

tick·​lish ˈti-k(ə-)lish 
Synonyms of ticklish
1
: sensitive to tickling
2
: requiring delicate handling
a ticklish subject
3
a
: touchy, oversensitive
ticklish about his baldness
b
: easily overturned
a canoe is a ticklish craft
ticklishly adverb
ticklishness noun

Synonyms

  • huffy
  • tetchy
  • thin-skinned
  • touchy
