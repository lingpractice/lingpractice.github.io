terrine

noun

ter·​rine tə-ˈrēn 
ter-ˈēn
1
a
: tureen sense 1
b
: a usually earthenware dish in which foods are cooked and served
2
: a mixture of chopped meat, fish, or vegetables cooked and served in a terrine
