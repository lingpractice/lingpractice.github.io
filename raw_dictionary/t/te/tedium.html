tedium

noun

te·​di·​um ˈtē-dē-əm 
Synonyms of tedium
1
: the quality or state of being tedious : tediousness
also : boredom
2
: a tedious period of time

Synonyms

  • blahs
  • boredom
  • doldrums
  • ennui
  • listlessness
  • restlessness
  • weariness
