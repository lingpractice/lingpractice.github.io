teleconferencing

noun

tele·​con·​fer·​enc·​ing ˈte-li-ˌkän-f(ə-)rən(t)-siŋ 
-fərn(t)-
: the holding of a conference among people remote from one another by means of telecommunication devices (such as telephones or computer terminals)
