thematic

adjective

the·​mat·​ic thi-ˈma-tik 
1
: of, relating to, or constituting a theme
2
a
: of or relating to the stem of a word
b
of a vowel : being the last part of a word stem before an inflectional ending
thematically
thi-ˈma-ti-k(ə-)lē 
adverb
