thermophilic

adjective

ther·​mo·​phil·​ic ˌthər-mə-ˈfi-lik 
variants or less commonly thermophilous
(ˌ)thər-ˈmä-fə-ləs 
or thermophile
ˈthər-mə-ˌfī(-ə)l 
: of, relating to, or being an organism living at a high temperature
thermophilic fermentation
thermophilic bacteria
thermophile noun
