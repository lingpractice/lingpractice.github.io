dimethyltryptamine

noun

di·​meth·​yl·​tryp·​ta·​mine ˌdī-ˌme-thəl-ˈtrip-tə-ˌmēn 
: a naturally occurring or easily synthesized hallucinogenic drug C12H16N2 that is chemically similar to but shorter acting than psilocybin

called also DMT
