dipteran

adjective

dip·​ter·​an ˈdip-tə-rən 
: of, relating to, or being a fly (see fly entry 4 sense 2a)
dipteran noun
dipterous
ˈdip-t(ə-)rəs 
adjective

Word History

Etymology

ultimately from Greek dipteros two-winged, from di- + pteron wing — more at feather

First Known Use

circa 1842, in the meaning defined above
