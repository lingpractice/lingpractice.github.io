Synonyms of dismount

transitive verb

1
: to throw down or remove from a mount or an elevated position
especially : unhorse
2
: disassemble

intransitive verb

1
obsolete : descend
2
: to alight from an elevated position (as on a horse)
also : to get out of an enclosed craft or vehicle
