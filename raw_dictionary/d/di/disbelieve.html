disbelieve

verb

dis·​be·​lieve ˌdis-bə-ˈlēv 
disbelieved; disbelieving; disbelieves
Synonyms of disbelieve

transitive verb

: to hold not worthy of belief : not believe

intransitive verb

: to withhold or reject belief
disbeliever noun

Synonyms

  • discredit
  • negate
