dignify

verb

dig·​ni·​fy ˈdig-nə-ˌfī 
dignified; dignifying
Synonyms of dignify

transitive verb

1
: to give distinction to : ennoble
2
: to confer dignity upon
also : to give undue attention or status to
won't dignify that remark with a reply
