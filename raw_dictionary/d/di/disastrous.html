disastrous

adjective

di·​sas·​trous di-ˈza-strəs 
 also  -ˈsa-
Synonyms of disastrous
1
: attended by or causing suffering or disaster : calamitous
a disastrous flood
2
: terrible, horrendous
a disastrous score
disastrously adverb
