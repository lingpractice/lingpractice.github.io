diestrus

noun

di·​es·​trus (ˌ)dī-ˈe-strəs 
: a period of sexual quiescence that intervenes between two periods of estrus
diestrous
(ˌ)dī-ˈe-strəs 
adjective

Word History

Etymology

New Latin, from dia- + estrus

First Known Use

1942, in the meaning defined above
