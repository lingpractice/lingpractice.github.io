discography

noun

dis·​cog·​ra·​phy di-ˈskä-grə-fē 
plural discographies
1
: a descriptive list of recordings by category, composer, performer, or date of release
an Elvis Presley discography
2
: the history of recorded music
discographical
ˌdi-skə-ˈgra-fi-kəl 
adjective
