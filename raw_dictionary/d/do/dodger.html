dodger

noun

dodg·​er ˈdä-jər 
Synonyms of dodger
1
: one that dodges
especially : one who uses tricky devices
2
: a small leaflet : circular
3
: corn dodger
4
: a usually canvas screen on a boat or ship that provides protection from spray
