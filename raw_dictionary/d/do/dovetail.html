dovetail

1 of 2

noun

dove·​tail ˈdəv-ˌtāl 
Synonyms of dovetail
: something resembling a dove's tail
especially : a flaring tenon and a mortise into which it fits tightly making an interlocking joint between two pieces (as of wood)
