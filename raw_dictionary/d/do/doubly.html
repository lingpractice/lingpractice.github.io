doubly

adverb

dou·​bly ˈdə-b(ə-)lē 
Synonyms of doubly
1
: in a twofold manner
2
: to twice the degree
doubly glad
doubly frustrating
3
: in two ways : for two reasons
The gentle rain was a doubly good thing because it washed the pollen off our cars.—Glynn Moore

Synonyms

  • double
  • twice
  • twofold
