dumbass

noun

dumb·​ass ˈdəm-ˌas 
variants or dumb-ass
plural dumbasses or dumb-asses
informal + vulgar
: a foolish or stupid person : idiot
acting like a total dumbass
dumbass adjective
or dumb-ass
informal + vulgar
This is a dumb-ass picture about dumb-ass men. —Anthony Lane
