duopoly

noun

du·​op·​o·​ly du̇-ˈä-pə-lē 
 also  dyu̇-
plural duopolies
1
: an oligopoly limited to two sellers
2
: preponderant influence or control by two political powers
duopolistic
d(y)u̇-ˌä-pə-ˈli-stik 
adjective
