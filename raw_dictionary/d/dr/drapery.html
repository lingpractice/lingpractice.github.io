drapery

noun

drap·​ery ˈdrā-p(ə-)rē 
plural draperies
Synonyms of drapery
1
British : dry goods
2
a
: a decorative piece of material usually hung in loose folds and arranged in a graceful design
b
: hangings of heavy fabric for use as a curtain
3
: the draping or arranging of materials

Synonyms

  • curtains
  • drapes
