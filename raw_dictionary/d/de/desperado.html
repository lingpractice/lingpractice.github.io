desperado

noun

des·​per·​a·​do ˌde-spə-ˈrä-(ˌ)dō 
-ˈrā-
plural desperadoes or desperados
Synonyms of desperado
: a bold or violent criminal
especially : a bandit of the western U.S. in the 19th century

Word History

Etymology

First Known Use
