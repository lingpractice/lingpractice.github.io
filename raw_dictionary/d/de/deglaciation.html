deglaciation

noun

de·​gla·​ci·​a·​tion (ˌ)dē-ˌglā-shē-ˈā-shən 
-sē-
: the melting of ice
specifically : the retreat of a glacier or ice sheet
deglaciated
(ˌ)dē-ˈglā-shē-ˌā-təd 
-sē-
adjective
