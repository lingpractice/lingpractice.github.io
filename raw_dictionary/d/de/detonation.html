detonation

noun

det·​o·​na·​tion ˌde-tᵊn-ˈā-shən 
ˌde-tə-ˈnā-
Synonyms of detonation
1
: the action or process of detonating
2
: rapid combustion in an internal combustion engine that results in knocking
