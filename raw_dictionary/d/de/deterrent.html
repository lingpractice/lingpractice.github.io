deterrent

adjective

-ˈter-;
-ˈtə-rənt,

Synonyms of deterrent
1
: serving to discourage, prevent, or inhibit : serving to deter
The ads had a deterrent effect on youth smoking.
2
: relating to deterrence
a deterrent view of punishment
deterrent noun
deterrently adverb
