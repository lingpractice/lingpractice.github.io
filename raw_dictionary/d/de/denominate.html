denominate

verb

de·​nom·​i·​nate di-ˈnä-mə-ˌnāt 
dē-
denominated; denominating; denominates
Synonyms of denominate

transitive verb

1
: to give a name to : designate
2
: to express or designate in some denomination
will denominate prices in U.S. dollars
