decoration

noun

dec·​o·​ra·​tion ˌde-kə-ˈrā-shən 
Synonyms of decoration
1
: the act or process of decorating
2
: something that adorns, enriches, or beautifies : ornament
3
: a badge of honor (such as a U.S. military award)
