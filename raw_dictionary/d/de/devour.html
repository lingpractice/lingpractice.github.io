devour

verb

de·​vour di-ˈvau̇(-ə)r 
dē-
devoured; devouring; devours
Synonyms of devour

transitive verb

1
: to eat up greedily or ravenously
devoured the turkey and mashed potatoes
2
: to use up or destroy as if by eating
We are devouring the world's resources.
3
: to prey upon
devoured by guilt
4
: to enjoy avidly
devours books
devourer noun

Synonyms

  • consume
  • eat (up)
