velvety

adjective

vel·​vety ˈvel-və-tē 
Synonyms of velvety
1
: having the character of velvet as in being soft, smooth, thick, or richly hued
velvety hair
a velvety green pasture
2
: smooth to the taste : mild
a velvety wine

Synonyms

  • cottony
  • downy
  • satin
