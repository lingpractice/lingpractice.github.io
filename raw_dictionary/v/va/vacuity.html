vacuity

noun

va·​cu·​i·​ty va-ˈkyü-ə-tē 
və-
plural vacuities
Synonyms of vacuity
1
: an empty space
2
: the state, fact, or quality of being vacuous
3
: something (such as an idea) that is vacuous or inane

Synonyms

  • black hole
  • blank
  • blankness
  • emptiness
  • vacancy
  • void
