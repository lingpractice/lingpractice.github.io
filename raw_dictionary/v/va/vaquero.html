vaquero

noun

va·​que·​ro vä-ˈker-(ˌ)ō 
plural vaqueros
Synonyms of vaquero
: herdsman, cowboy
—used in reference to cowboys in areas (such as Mexico and the southwestern U.S.) where Spanish is spoken
