vocational

adjective

vo·​ca·​tion·​al
vō-ˈkā-sh(ə-)nəl
 
1
: of, relating to, or concerned with a vocation
2
: of, relating to, or undergoing training in a skill or trade to be pursued as a career
a vocational school
vocational students
vocationally adverb
