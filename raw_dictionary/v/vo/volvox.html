volvox

noun

vol·​vox ˈväl-ˌväks 
ˈvȯl-
: any of a genus (Volvox) of flagellated unicellular green algae that form spherical colonies

Word History

Etymology

New Latin, from Latin volvere to roll

First Known Use

1798, in the meaning defined above
