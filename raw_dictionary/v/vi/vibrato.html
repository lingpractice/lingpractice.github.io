vibrato

noun

vi·​bra·​to vi-ˈbrä-(ˌ)tō 
vī-
plural vibratos
: a slightly tremulous effect imparted to vocal or instrumental tone for added warmth and expressiveness by slight and rapid variations in pitch
vibratoless
vi-ˈbrä-(ˌ)tō-ləs 
vī-
adjective
