vindictive

adjective

vin·​dic·​tive vin-ˈdik-tiv 
Synonyms of vindictive
1
a
: disposed to seek revenge : vengeful
b
: intended for or involving revenge
2
: intended to cause anguish or hurt : spiteful
vindictively adverb
vindictiveness noun

Synonyms
