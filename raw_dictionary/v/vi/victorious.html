victorious

adjective

vic·​to·​ri·​ous vik-ˈtȯr-ē-əs 
Synonyms of victorious
1
a
: having won a victory
a victorious army
b
: of, relating to, or characteristic of victory
victorious exuberance
2
: evincing moral harmony or a sense of fulfillment : fulfilled
victoriously adverb
victoriousness noun
