monounsaturated

adjective

mono·​un·​sat·​u·​rat·​ed ˌmä-nō-ˌən-ˈsa-chə-ˌrā-təd 
: containing one double or triple bond per molecule
—used especially of an oil, fat, or fatty acid compare polyunsaturated
monounsaturate
ˌmä-nō-ˌən-ˈsa-chə-rət 
noun
