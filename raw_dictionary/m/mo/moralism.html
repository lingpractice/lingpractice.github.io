moralism

noun

mor·​al·​ism ˈmȯr-ə-ˌli-zəm 
ˈmär-
Synonyms of moralism
1
a
: the habit or practice of moralizing
b
: a conventional moral attitude or saying
2
: an often exaggerated emphasis on morality (as in politics)

Synonyms

  • nice-nellyism
  • prudery
  • prudishness
  • puritanism
