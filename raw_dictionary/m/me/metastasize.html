metastasize

verb

me·​tas·​ta·​size mə-ˈta-stə-ˌsīz 
metastasized; metastasizing

intransitive verb

: to spread or grow by or as if by metastasis
died of lung cancer which had metastasized throughout his body—G. P. Elliott
