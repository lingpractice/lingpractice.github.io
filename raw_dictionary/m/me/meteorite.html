meteorite

noun

me·​te·​or·​ite ˈmē-tē-ə-ˌrīt 
: a meteor that reaches the surface of the earth without being completely vaporized
meteoritic
ˌmē-tē-ə-ˈri-tik 
adjective
or less commonly meteoritical
ˌmē-tē-ə-ˈri-ti-kəl
