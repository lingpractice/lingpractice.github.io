medievalism

noun

me·​di·​e·​val·​ism ˌmē-ˈdē-və-ˌli-zəm 
mi-,
ˌme-,
-dē-ˈē-və-
1
: medieval quality, character, or state
2
: devotion to the institutions, arts, and practices of the Middle Ages
