metronidazole

noun

met·​ro·​ni·​da·​zole ˌme-trə-ˈnī-də-ˌzōl 
: a drug C6H9N3O3 used especially to treat vaginal trichomoniasis, amebiasis, and infections by anaerobic bacteria
