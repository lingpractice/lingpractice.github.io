messianic

adjective

mes·​si·​an·​ic ˌme-sē-ˈa-nik 
Synonyms of messianic
1
: of or relating to a messiah
this messianic kingdom
2
: marked by idealism and an aggressive crusading spirit
messianic zeal
on a messianic mission
