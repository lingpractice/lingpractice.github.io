megabyte

noun

mega·​byte ˈme-gə-ˌbīt 
: 1024 kilobytes or 1,048,576 bytes
also : one million bytes

Word History

Etymology

from the fact that 1,048,576 (220) is the power of 2 closest to one million

First Known Use

1965, in the meaning defined above
