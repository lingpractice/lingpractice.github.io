mesa

noun

me·​sa ˈmā-sə 
Synonyms of mesa
: an isolated relatively flat-topped natural elevation usually more extensive than a butte and less extensive than a plateau
also : a broad terrace with an abrupt slope on one side : bench

Synonyms

  • altiplano
  • plateau
  • table
  • tableland
