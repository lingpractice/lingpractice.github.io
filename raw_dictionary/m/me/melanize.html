melanize

verb

mel·​a·​nize ˈme-lə-ˌnīz 
melanized; melanizing

transitive verb

1
: to convert into or infiltrate with melanin
2
: to make dark or black
melanization
ˌme-lə-nə-ˈzā-shən 
noun

Word History

Etymology

melan(in) + -ize

First Known Use

1967, in the meaning defined at sense 1
