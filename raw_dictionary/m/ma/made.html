made

2 of 2

adjective

ˈmād 
1
a
: fictitious, invented
a made excuse
b
: artificially produced
c
: put together of various ingredients
a made dish
2
: assured of success
a made man
—usually used in the phrase have it made

Word History

Etymology

Adjective

Middle English, from past participle of maken to make

First Known Use

Adjective

14th century, in the meaning defined at sense 1a
