magus

noun

ma·​gus ˈmā-gəs 
plural magi ˈmā-ˌjī 
ˈma-
Synonyms of magus
1
a
: a member of a hereditary priestly class among the ancient Medes and Persians
b
often capitalized : one of the traditionally three wise men from the East paying homage to the infant Jesus
2
: magician, sorcerer
