marten

noun

mar·​ten ˈmär-tᵊn 
plural marten or martens
Synonyms of marten
1
: any of several semiarboreal slender-bodied carnivorous mammals (genus Martes) of the weasel family that occur in the northern hemisphere
2
: the fur or pelt of a marten
