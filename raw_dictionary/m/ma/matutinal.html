matutinal

adjective

ma·​tu·​ti·​nal ˌma-chu̇-ˈtī-nᵊl 
mə-ˈtüt-nəl,
-ˈtyüt-,
-ˈtü-tə-nəl 
-ˈtyü-
: of, relating to, or occurring in the morning : early
matutinally adverb

Word History

Etymology

Late Latin matutinalis, from Latin matutinus — more at matinee

First Known Use

1567, in the meaning defined above
