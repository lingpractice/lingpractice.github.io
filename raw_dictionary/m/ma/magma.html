magma

noun

mag·​ma ˈmag-mə 
1
archaic : dregs, sediment
2
: a thin pasty suspension (as of a precipitate in water)
3
: molten rock material within the earth from which igneous rock results by cooling
magmatic
mag-ˈma-tik 
adjective
