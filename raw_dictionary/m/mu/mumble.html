mumble

verb

mum·​ble ˈməm-bəl 
mumbled; mumbling ˈməm-b(ə-)liŋ 
Synonyms of mumble

intransitive verb

: to utter words in a low confused indistinct manner : mutter

transitive verb

1
: to utter with a low inarticulate voice
2
: to chew or bite with or as if with toothless gums
