multigrid

adjective

mul·​ti·​grid ˌməl-tē-ˈgrid 
-ˌtī-
mathematics
: relating to or being any of multiple stepwise methods of refining an approximate solution to a differential equation by mapping the error of the approximate solution onto a progressively coarser grid (see grid sense 2b)

Word History

First Known Use

1977, in the meaning defined above
