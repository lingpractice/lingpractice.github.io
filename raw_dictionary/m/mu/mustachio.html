mustachio

noun

mus·​ta·​chio (ˌ)mə-ˈsta-shē-ˌō  -ˈstä- 
-shō
variants or less commonly moustachio
plural mustachios
: mustache
especially : a large mustache
mustachioed adjective
or less commonly moustachioed
(ˌ)mə-ˈsta-shē-ˌōd 
-ˈstä-,
-shōd

Word History

Etymology

Spanish & Italian; Spanish mostacho, from Italian mustaccio, from Old Italian

First Known Use

1603, in the meaning defined above
