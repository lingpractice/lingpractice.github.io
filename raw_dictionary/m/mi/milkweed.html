milkweed

noun

milk·​weed ˈmilk-ˌwēd 
: any of various plants that secrete latex
especially : any of a genus (Asclepias of the family Asclepiadaceae, the milkweed family) of erect chiefly perennial herbs with milky juice and umbellate flowers
