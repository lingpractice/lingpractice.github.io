militarize

verb

mil·​i·​ta·​rize ˈmi-lə-tə-ˌrīz 
militarized; militarizing
Synonyms of militarize

transitive verb

1
: to give a military character to
2
: to equip with military forces and defenses
3
: to adapt for military use
militarization
ˌmi-lə-t(ə-)rə-ˈzā-shən 
noun
