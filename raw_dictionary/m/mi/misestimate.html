misestimate

verb

mis·​es·​ti·​mate ˌmis-ˈe-stə-ˌmāt 
misestimated; misestimating
Synonyms of misestimate

transitive verb

: to estimate (something) wrongly
He reopens one of the epic disputes of the cold war, namely whether the C.I.A. misestimated Soviet military and economic strength in the 1970's …—Philip Taubman
misestimate
ˌmis-ˈe-stə-mət 
noun
plural misestimates
His misestimate was big but inconsequential, at least in the short term. —Ross Ramsey
misestimation
ˌmis-ˌe-stə-ˈmā-shən 
noun
plural misestimations
a serious misestimation of the charges
