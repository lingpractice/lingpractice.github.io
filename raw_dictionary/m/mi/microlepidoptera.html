microlepidoptera

plural noun

mi·​cro·​lep·​i·​dop·​tera ˌmī-krō-ˌle-pə-ˈdäp-tə-rə 
: lepidopterous insects (such as tortricids) that belong to families of minute or medium-sized moths
microlepidopterous
ˌmī-krō-ˌle-pə-ˈdäp-tə-rəs 
adjective
